核心观点 · Key points

  • Meta 是一家专注于人类连接的科技公司，而非特定的应用或社交网络。
    Meta is a technology company focused on human connection, not a specific app or social network.
  • Meta 成功的关键在于成为一家强大的科技公司，拥有快速迭代的学习文化。
    The key to Meta's success is being a strong technology company with a learning culture that iterates quickly.
  • Meta 的治理结构（包括超级投票权）允许长期思考和战略押注。
    Meta's governance structure, including super voting shares, allows long-term thinking and strategic bets.
  • 开源对 Meta 具有战略价值，能够建立行业标准并节省成本。
    Open source is strategically valuable for Meta, enabling industry standards and cost savings.
  • Meta 旨在构建下一代开放平台，与苹果的封闭模式竞争。
    Meta aims to build the next generation of open platforms, competing with Apple's closed model.
  • 终极社交体验是通过眼镜实现全息临场感，结合 AR 和 AI。
    The ultimate social experience is holographic presence through glasses, combining AR and AI.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 扎克伯格后悔接受了他人关于 Meta 政治影响的叙事，称其为 20 年的错误。
    Mark regrets accepting others' narratives about Meta's political impact, calling it a 20-year mistake.
  • Meta 的移动 HTML5 策略是失败的，但市值腰斩的痛苦是值得的。
    Meta's mobile HTML5 strategy was a failure, but the pain of losing half market cap was worth it.
  • 扎克伯格最初并未打算创办公司；Facebook 是一个项目，后来变成了公司。
    Mark didn't initially intend to start a company; Facebook was a project that became a company.
  • Meta 对 Reality Labs 的投资是合理的，因为它能控制自己的平台并打造令人敬畏的产品。
    Meta's investment in Reality Labs is justified by controlling its own platform and enabling awesome products.
  • 扎克伯格认为开放平台可以战胜封闭平台，这与 iPhone 的主导地位相反。
    Mark believes open platforms can win over closed ones, contrary to the iPhone's dominance.
  • 扎克伯格设计衬衫和养牛等个人追求是打造令人敬畏事物的一部分。
    Mark's personal pursuits like designing shirts and raising cattle are part of building awesome things.
