Mang 讨论了追逐潮流如何导致 AI 垃圾，并分享了他从 Cursor 转向 Codeex 来构建应用和设计的经历，强调需要领先于基准线。

Mang discusses how chasing trends leads to AI slop, and shares his shift from Cursor to Codeex for building apps and designs, emphasizing the need to stay ahead of the baseline.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 设计师必须用AI构建自己的工具，以免被困在云端产品中。
    Designers must build their own tools with AI to avoid being trapped in cloud-based products.
  • 使用截图作为起点是无需打字或语音的最快构建方式。
    Using screenshots as starting points is the fastest way to build without typing or voicing.
  • design.md文件封装了设计师的风格指南，供AI读取和应用。
    A design.md file encapsulates a designer's style guide for AI to read and apply.
  • 最优秀的人是编辑者，而非从零开始；他们从参考中迭代。
    The best people are editors, not starting from scratch; they iterate from references.
  • 要充分利用强大模型，必须提供包含上下文和设计系统的巨大提示。
    To fully utilize powerful models, you must give them a giant prompt with context and design system.
  • 随着AI工具快速演进，好奇心和持续优化工作流程至关重要。
    Curiosity and constant workflow refinement are essential as AI tools evolve rapidly.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 如果你总是追逐潮流，你很快就会变成AI垃圾。
    If you always chase trends, you will become AI slop very soon.
  • AI并没有变得更聪明；你需要巨大的提示才能利用超级智能模型。
    AI is not getting any smarter; you need a giant prompt to utilize super intelligent models.
  • 领先的唯一方法是将最后10%的精力花在现有库中没有的独特功能上。
    The only way to be ahead is to spend that last 10% on unique features not in existing libraries.
  • 你不需要提前计划一切；独自工作时，探索就是你的超能力。
    You don't need to plan everything in advance; working solo, discovery is your superpower.
  • 我现在写0%的代码；AI写所有代码，但你必须精确沟通。
    I write 0% of the code now; AI writes everything, but you must be precise in communication.
  • 用视频教AI如何提示动画比学习动画术语更有效。
    Using a video to teach AI how to prompt an animation is more effective than learning animation terms.
