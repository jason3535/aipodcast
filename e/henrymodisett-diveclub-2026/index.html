本期速览 · Overview

Perplexity 设计副总裁 Henry Modiset 探讨了他们的设计文化和品牌叙事如何吸引顶尖人才，以及从初创公司发展为广泛使用的应用所面临的挑战。

Henry Modiset, VP of Design at Perplexity, discusses how their design culture and brand narrative attract top talent, and the challenges of evolving from a startup to a widely-used app.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 设计是战略武器，不只是美学。
    Design is a strategic weapon for winning, not just aesthetics.
  • 招聘多样原型而非克隆体，以提升团队能力。
    Hire diverse archetypes, not clones, to boost team capability.
  • 快速发布并迭代；速度胜过完美。
    Ship fast and iterate; velocity beats perfection.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 设计是战略武器；创始人必须阐明设计如何帮助公司获胜。
    Design is a strategic weapon; founders must articulate why design helps the company win.
  • 招聘多样化技能和类型的人，而非克隆体，以提升团队能力和协作。
    Hire for diverse skills and archetypes, not clones, to increase team capability and collaboration.
  • 速度和果断比完美更重要；快速发布并迭代。
    Velocity and decisiveness are more important than perfection; ship fast and iterate.
  • 品牌传递质量信号并吸引人才；早期投资品牌以脱颖而出。
    Brand signals quality and attracts talent; invest in brand early to stand out.
  • 给予设计师所有权和自主权；让他们做决策以成长。
    Give designers ownership and autonomy; let them make decisions to grow.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 人们加入的是品牌，而非公司；品牌叙事是招聘工具。
    People join a brand, not a company; brand narrative is a recruiting tool.
  • 避免结构化的设计评审；偏好有机、按需的反馈以保持速度。
    Avoid structured design crits; prefer organic, on-demand feedback to stay fast.
  • 招聘那些在作业之外自学和发明的实习生，而不仅仅是好学生。
    Hire interns who self-learn and invent outside assignments, not just good students.
  • 设计流程应适应问题，而非遵循通用模板。
    Design process should adapt to the problem, not follow a universal template.
  • 在快速增长的初创公司，直觉决策往往胜过数据驱动。
    In a fast-growing startup, gut decisions often beat data-driven ones.
