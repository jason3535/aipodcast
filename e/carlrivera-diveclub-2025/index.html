本期速览 · Overview

Shopify 首席设计官 Carl Rivera 探讨了从第一性原理重新构想电商平台的使命，利用 AI 和设计创造真正的原生体验。

Shopify's Chief Design Officer Carl Rivera discusses the mission to reimagine the e-commerce platform from first principles, leveraging AI and design to create truly native experiences.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 在 AI 时代，设计是软件的关键差异化因素，而不仅仅是实用性。

核心观点 · Key points

  • 在AI时代，设计是软件的关键差异化因素，而不仅仅是实用性。
    Design is the key differentiator for software in the AI era, not just utility.
  • 聚焦关键路径和工艺，打造用户愿意回归的优质体验。
    Focus on hot paths and craft to create premium experiences users return to.
  • 设计师应上下兼顾：深耕设计系统并打磨关键路径。
    Designers should go up and down the stack: double down on design systems and craft hot paths.
  • 消除抽象层；在构建工具中审查作品，而非通过演示。
    Remove abstraction layers; review work in the tool it was built in, not presentations.
  • AI让每个人在每个岗位上达到7/10；设计师必须超越，达到10/10。
    AI makes everyone a 7/10 at every job; designers must push beyond that to 10/10.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 上下文很大程度上被高估了；设计师可以非常快速地接手新问题。
    Context is largely overrated; designers can onboard to new problems very fast.
  • 设计师不应以商业影响来证明工艺；相信品味和观点。
    Designers should not justify craft by business impact; trust taste and point of view.
  • 大多数产品组织的时间框架是错误的；聚焦极长期和极短期，砍掉中间。
    Most product organizations get time frames wrong; focus on very long-term and very short-term, cut the middle.
  • 由于生成能力，代理机构设计师可能比大厂设计师更受欢迎。
    Agency designers may be more sought after than those from big tech due to generative skills.
  • 软件将免费；差异化在于与用户建立关系的设计。
    Software will be free; the differentiator is design that builds a relationship with users.
