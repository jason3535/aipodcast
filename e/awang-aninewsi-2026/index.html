本期速览 · Overview

关于 AI 技术演变的讨论，从预训练到强化学习再到递归自我改进，代理在 2025 年成为现实。

A discussion on the evolution of AI technology, from pre-training to reinforcement learning and now recursive self-improvement, with agents becoming a reality in 2025.

要点 · TL;DR

  • AI 正进入递归自我改进的新时代，发展速度加快。
    AI is entering a new era of recursive self-improvement, accelerating development velocity.
  • 信任对 AI 采用至关重要；政府和公司必须合作进行安全测试。
    Trust is crucial for AI adoption; governments and companies must collaborate on safety testing.

核心观点 · Key points

  • AI 正进入递归自我改进的新时代，开发速度正在加速。
    AI is entering a new era of recursive self-improvement, accelerating development velocity.
  • 智能体现已真正发挥作用，从编码开始扩展到个人智能体。
    Agents are now working in earnest, starting with coding and expanding to personal agents.
  • 信任对 AI 采用至关重要；政府和公司必须合作进行安全测试。
    Trust is crucial for AI adoption; governments and companies must collaborate on safety testing.
  • 数据是新的石油；政府应将医疗和国家安全数据视为战略资产。
    Data is the new oil; governments should treat healthcare and national security data as strategic assets.
  • 在公共服务中部署 AI 可以展示切实利益并建立信任。
    AI deployment in public services can demonstrate tangible benefits and build trust.
  • 组织必须成为 AI 优先，自上而下指令与自下而上成功案例结合。
    Organizations must become AI-first, with top-down mandate and bottom-up success stories.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 缩放定律持续；关于收益递减的怀疑是错误的。
    Scaling laws continue; skepticism about diminishing returns was wrong.
  • 递归自我改进标志着超越预训练和强化学习的新范式。
    Recursive self-improvement marks a new paradigm beyond pre-training and reinforcement learning.
  • 经过多年炒作，智能体终于在 2025 年底开始真正工作。
    Agents are finally working after years of hype, starting in late 2025.
  • 印度的消费 AI 初创公司比美国还多，挑战了西方主导的观念。
    India has more consumer AI startups than the US, challenging the notion of Western dominance.
  • 技术领导力取决于部署而非发明；印度是典范。
    Technology leadership depends on deployment, not invention; India exemplifies this.
  • 政治领袖必须将 AI 视为当务之急，而非明日议题。
    Political leaders must treat AI as an immediate priority, not a tomorrow issue.
