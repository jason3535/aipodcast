本期速览 · Overview

Lovable 的第一位设计师 Nad Chishtie 分享了他从用 LLM 进行黑客实验到成为欧洲增长最快初创公司的创始设计师的历程，以及像 Lovable 这样的工具如何赋能设计师成为构建者。

Nad Chishtie, the first designer at Lovable, shares his journey from hacking with LLMs to becoming a founding designer at Europe's fastest-growing startup, and how tools like Lovable empower designers to become builders.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 初创公司的首位设计师应成为赋能者，而非瓶颈。
    First designer at a startup should be an enabler, not a bottleneck.
  • 质量如同债务，会波动，需迭代偿还。
    Quality is debt that fluctuates and is paid off iteratively.
  • AI 是创意伙伴，应开放地与其即兴协作，不拘泥于结构。
    AI is a creative partner; jam with it openly without rigid structure.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 设计师应成为赋能者而非瓶颈，通过引导工程师和统一问题认知来工作。
    Designers should be enablers, not bottlenecks, by guiding engineers and aligning on problems.
  • 质量如同债务：它会波动，需要与技术债务一起迭代偿还。
    Quality is like debt: it fluctuates, and you pay it off iteratively alongside technical debt.
  • 通过快速、粗糙的实验和短迭代周期最大化学习。
    Maximize learning through rapid, scrappy experimentation and short iteration cycles.
  • 将AI视为创意伙伴；开放地与其即兴协作，无需僵化结构。
    Treat AI as a creative partner; jam with it openly without rigid structure.
  • 设计工具的未来是互操作的，以代码为真相来源。
    The future of design tools is interoperable, with code as the source of truth.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 90年代的设计工具（图层面板）已成遗留；AI能带来真正的进步。
    Design tools from the 90s (layers panel) are legacy; AI enables genuine forward progress.
  • 用户愿意自学技术概念，只要他们感到被赋能。
    Users are willing to self-educate on technical concepts if they feel empowered.
  • 过于远见并押注未来模型能力可能导致失败。
    Being too visionary and betting on future model capabilities can lead to failure.
  • 在AI优先的公司中，整个职能（产品经理、研究、销售）可以合并消失。
    Entire functions (PM, research, sales) can collapse and merge in AI-first companies.
  • 设计与工程是一体两面；AI进一步融合它们。
    Design and engineering are two sides of the same coin; AI merges them further.
