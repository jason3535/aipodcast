本期速览 · Overview

Anthropic 的 Jenny Wen 讨论了三种她期待雇佣的设计师类型：块状设计师、深度茶专家和疯狂的新毕业生。

Jenny Wen from Anthropic discusses three types of designers she's excited to hire: the block-shaped designer, the deep tea specialist, and the cracked new grad.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 设计流程已死，快速构建和交付才是关键。
    Design process is dead; build and ship fast.
  • 三种设计师原型：块状、深 T 型、破解型新人。
    Three designer archetypes: block-shaped, deep T, cracked new grad.
  • 设计师掌控 AI 采用节奏，新工具等 6 个月再用。
    Designers control AI adoption; wait 6 months before adopting new tools.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 设计流程已死；设计师必须快速构建和交付，而非依赖旧方法。
    Design process is dead; designers must build and ship fast, not rely on old methods.
  • 三种设计师原型：块状型、深T型专家和疯狂的新毕业生。
    Three designer archetypes: block-shaped, deep T specialist, and cracked new grad.
  • 仅凭品味无法拯救设计师；判断力、权威和愿景才是关键。
    Taste alone won't save designers; judgment, authority, and vision are key.
  • 设计师控制AI进入工作的程度；采用新工具前等待6个月。
    Designers control how much AI enters their work; wait 6 months before adopting new tools.
  • 将工作和用户置于中心，而非AI炒作周期。
    Put the work and users at the center, not the AI hype cycle.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • Figma在探索设计变体方面仍比用AI编码更快。
    Figma is still faster for exploring design variations than coding with AI.
  • 设计师应雇佣不受旧流程束缚的疯狂新毕业生。
    Designers should hire cracked new grads who aren't burdened by old processes.
  • 北极星目标现在只有3-6个月，而非数年，即使对前沿公司也是如此。
    North stars are now 3-6 months out, not years, even for cutting-edge companies.
  • 设计师的独特价值在于容忍模糊性，而不仅仅是以人为中心。
    Designers' unique value is sitting with ambiguity, not just human-centeredness.
  • 速度并非改进；'人人都在用'不是好的设计论据。
    Speed is not an improvement; 'everyone is using it' is not a good design argument.
