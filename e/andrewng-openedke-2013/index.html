用在线课程扩大教育规模

Scaling Education with Online Courses

本期速览 · Overview

一位斯坦福教授兼在线教育平台联合创始人分享他们如何将一门400人的课程扩展到10万在线学生，如今服务全球超过500万学生。

A Stanford professor and co-founder of an online education platform shares how they scaled from a 400-student class to 100,000 students online, and now serve over 5 million students worldwide.

要点 · TL;DR

  • Coursera 旨在通过可扩展的在线课程，让教育成为一项基本人权。
    Coursera aims to make education a fundamental human right through scalable online courses.
  • 自动评分和同伴互评在大规模上实现了掌握学习。
    Mastery learning is enabled by auto-grading and peer grading at scale.
  • 翻转课堂模式利用在线内容增强校园互动。
    Flipped classroom model uses online content to enhance on-campus interaction.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 教育应是一项基本人权，而不仅仅是特权阶层的权利。
    Education should be a fundamental human right, not just for the privileged.
  • 带有自动评分和同伴评分的在线课程能够大规模实现掌握学习。
    Online courses with auto-grading and peer grading enable mastery learning at scale.
  • 翻转课堂模式利用在线内容释放课堂时间，用于更深入的互动。
    Flipped classroom model uses online content to free up class time for deeper interaction.
  • 来自数百万学生的数据可以通过统计调整提高评分准确性。
    Data from millions of students can improve grading accuracy through statistical adjustments.
  • 结合打字生物特征和照片ID的验证证书提供了可信的凭证。
    Verified certificates with typing biometrics and photo ID provide credible credentials.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 大学的角色不仅仅是传播内容，而是促进互动。
    University's role is not just to broadcast content, but to foster interactions.
  • 大规模同伴评分经过数学修正后可以与教师评分一样准确。
    Peer grading at scale can be as accurate as teacher grading with mathematical corrections.
  • 免费在线课程也可以通过翻转课堂改善校园教育。
    Free online courses can also improve on-campus education via the flipped classroom.
  • 大多数Coursera学生已经拥有学士学位，并非传统大学适龄人群。
    Most Coursera students already have a bachelor's degree, not traditional college-age.
  • 内容许可费可能是维持大规模免费教育所必需的。
    Content licensing fees may be necessary to sustain free education at scale.
