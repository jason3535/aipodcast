一位斯坦福教授兼在线教育平台联合创始人分享他们如何将一门 400 人的课程扩展到 10 万在线学生，如今服务全球超过 500 万学生。

A Stanford professor and co-founder of an online education platform shares how they scaled from a 400-student class to 100,000 students online, and now serve over 5 million students worldwide.
