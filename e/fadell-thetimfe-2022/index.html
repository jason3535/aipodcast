本期速览 · Overview

托尼·法德尔解释新书《Build》如何源于多年指导创业者的经历，将日常建议转化为适合各年龄段的“盒中导师”。

Tony Fadell explains how his new book 'Build' emerged from years of mentoring startup founders, turning daily advice into a mentor-in-a-box for all ages.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 先写新闻稿再开发，用故事定义产品。
    Write the press release before building to define the product's story.
  • 全球 60%的能源被浪费，解决能效是气候问题的关键。
    Energy inefficiency causes 60% loss globally; targeting it is key.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 故事讲述应定义产品，而非仅用于销售。在构建产品前先写好新闻稿。
    Storytelling should define the product, not just sell it. Write the press release before building.
  • 全球能源效率低下导致60%的损失；解决这一问题是气候方案的关键。
    Energy inefficiency causes 60% loss globally; targeting it is key to climate solutions.
  • 小团队通过自我管理、任务轮换和明确优先级来避免倦怠。
    Small teams thrive on self-management, task rotation, and clear prioritization to avoid burnout.
  • 乐观通过改变媒体摄入和关注解决方案而非问题来培养。
    Optimism is cultivated by changing media diet and focusing on solutions, not problems.
  • 氢经济和生物质经济是工业脱碳中投资不足的领域。
    Hydrogen and biomass economies are underinvested areas for decarbonizing industry.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 被称为‘混蛋’可能意味着你深切关心使命和质量，而非自我。
    Being called an 'asshole' can mean you care deeply about mission and quality, not ego.
  • 史蒂夫·乔布斯的假期是紧张的头脑风暴，而非真正的休息。
    Steve Jobs' vacations were intense brainstorming sessions, not true time off.
  • 今天的通胀实际上是外包外部性导致通缩结束的结果。
    Inflation today is actually the end of deflation from outsourcing externalities.
  • 首位万亿富翁将解决气候问题，因为生存是最大的痛点。
    The first trillionaire will solve climate problems because survival is the ultimate pain.
  • 技术可以打破世界秩序的历史周期，改变可预测的轨迹。
    Technology can disrupt historical cycles of world order, changing predictable arcs.
