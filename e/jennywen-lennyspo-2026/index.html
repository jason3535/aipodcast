AI 如何重塑设计流程

How AI is Reshaping the Design Process

本期速览 · Overview

设计师正从制作精美原型转向协助工程师执行，AI 加速了节奏，将设计周期从数年缩短至数月。

Designers are shifting from creating polished mocks to helping engineers execute, with AI accelerating the pace and reducing the design horizon from years to months.

要点 · TL;DR

  • AI 终结了传统设计流程，设计师转而协助工程师执行。
    AI kills traditional design process; designers now help engineers execute.
  • 设计师花在原型上的时间从 60-70% 降至 30-40%。
    Designers spend 30-40% time mocking up, down from 60-70%.
  • 通过速度建立信任：尽早发布、快速迭代、响应反馈。
    Trust built through speed: ship early, iterate fast, respond to feedback.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 传统的设计流程（研究、发散、收敛）因AI而消亡。
    The traditional design process of research, diverge, converge is dead due to AI.
  • 设计师现在花30-40%的时间做原型，低于之前的60-70%。
    Designers now spend 30-40% of time mocking up, down from 60-70%.
  • 设计师必须帮助工程师执行，而不仅仅是交付原型。
    Designers must help engineers execute, not just hand off mocks.
  • 设计愿景现在展望3-6个月，而非2-5年。
    Design vision now looks 3-6 months out, not 2-5 years.
  • 通过速度建立信任：尽早发布、快速迭代、响应反馈。
    Building trust through speed: ship early, iterate fast, respond to feedback.
  • AI在品味和判断力上会进步，但人类仍需决定什么重要。
    AI will improve at taste and judgment, but humans still decide what matters.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 设计管理者应回归个人贡献者角色以理解变化中的流程。
    Design managers should return to IC to understand changing processes.
  • 高级领导者做低杠杆任务可能变成高杠杆。
    Low-leverage tasks by senior leaders can be high-leverage.
  • 团队互相调侃是心理安全的标志，而非功能失调。
    Team roasting is a sign of psychological safety, not dysfunction.
  • 招聘“优秀新毕业生”比有经验的高级人才更好。
    Hire 'crack new grads' with blank slates over experienced seniors.
  • AI的聊天和终端界面将持续存在，不会被图形界面取代。
    Chat and terminal UIs for AI will persist, not be replaced by graphical UIs.
  • 设计师应是“块状”通才，而不仅是T型人才。
    Designers should be 'block-shaped' generalists, not just T-shaped.
