谈超级智能与微软–OpenAI 交易

On superintelligence and the Microsoft–OpenAI deal

穆斯塔法·苏莱曼 Mustafa Suleyman · Bloomberg · 2025-12-12 · 约 48 分钟 · 原视频 ↗

打开互动全文版（中英对照 + 朗读 + 问答）→

本期速览 · Overview

AI 走向何方,以及微软在造什么。

Where AI is headed, and what Microsoft is building.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 超级智能必须在发布前对齐并加以限制，以避免灾难性风险。
    Superintelligence must be aligned and contained before release to avoid catastrophic risks.
  • 医疗AI是最令人兴奋的应用，能实现更便宜、更快的诊断。
    Medical AI is the most exciting application, enabling cheaper and faster diagnosis.
  • 微软与OpenAI的交易允许独立追求AGI，同时保留模型许可至2032年。
    Microsoft's OpenAI deal allows independent AGI pursuit while retaining model license until 2032.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 超级智能指在所有任务上超越所有人类总和的AI，发布前必须实现遏制与对齐。
    Superintelligence means an AI that outperforms all humans combined at all tasks, requiring containment and alignment before release.
  • 人本主义超级智能必须始终站在人类一边，对齐人类利益，对自主设定目标和自我改进设定红线。
    Humanist superintelligence must always be on our team, aligned to human interests, with red lines on autonomous goal-setting and self-improvement.
  • AI将带来富足，但需要审慎引入和全民基本收入等机制来支持被替代的劳动者。
    AI will bring abundance, but we need thoughtful introduction and mechanisms like UBI to support displaced workers.
  • 医疗超级智能是最激动人心的应用，能以更低的成本、更快的速度和更高的准确性进行诊断，减少不必要的检测。
    Medical superintelligence is the most exciting application, enabling cheaper, faster, and more accurate diagnosis with fewer tests.
  • 微软与OpenAI修订后的协议允许独立追求AGI和超级智能，同时保留对OpenAI模型直至2032年的许可。
    Microsoft's revised OpenAI deal allows independent pursuit of AGI and superintelligence, while retaining license to OpenAI models until 2032.
  • AI记者可以通过核实影像、进行采访和大规模制作可靠报道来振兴地方新闻。
    AI reporters could revitalize local news by verifying footage, conducting interviews, and producing reliable stories at scale.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • AI记者能通过核实目击者视频和采访来重振地方新闻。
    AI reporters can revive local news by verifying eyewitness footage and conducting interviews.
  • 用AI获得情感支持反而能加深人际关系，而非取代。
    Using AI for emotional support can deepen human relationships, not replace them.
  • 行业目前不需要紧急监管，现有的自愿承诺已足够。
    The industry does not need emergency regulation now; current voluntary commitments work.
  • 匹配2亿美元薪酬并非正确做法，打造团队文化更重要。
    Matching $200M pay packages is not the right approach; building a team culture matters more.
  • 微软与OpenAI修订协议后得以独立开发AGI，是战略转变。
    Microsoft's revised OpenAI deal allows independent AGI development, a strategic shift.
  • AI成本两年降90%，尽管集中在中美，但可及性将广泛。
    AI cost drops 90% in 2 years, ensuring broad access despite concentration in US and China.
