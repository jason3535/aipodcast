本期速览 · Overview

托尼和哈里讨论童年时期 15 年内搬家 12 次的影响，如何塑造韧性，以及这段经历如何影响今天的育儿方式，包括跨国搬家以及教导孩子适应变化的重要性。

Tony and Harry discuss the impact of moving 12 times in 15 years during childhood, how it shaped resilience, and how that experience influences parenting today, including moving between countries and the importance of teaching kids to adapt to change.

  • 适应变化的能力至关重要；父母应教孩子适应全球化世界。

核心观点 · Key points

  • 适应变化的能力至关重要；父母应教导孩子适应全球化的世界。
    Resilience to change is crucial; parents should teach kids to adapt to a globalized world.
  • 通过展示价值创造自己的角色；如果受阻，就离开去创业。
    Create your own role by showing value; if blocked, leave and start a startup.
  • 最好的领导者扮演教练角色，而非仅仅是CEO或家长，平衡团队与个人成长。
    The best leaders act as coaches, not just CEOs or parents, balancing team and individual growth.
  • 在缺乏数据时基于观点投资；在别人扎堆时另辟蹊径，寻找未来机会。
    Invest based on opinion when data is absent; zig while others zag to find future opportunities.
  • 混合办公是未来；领导者必须修复文化以吸引人们回归，而非强迫。
    Hybrid work is the future; leaders must fix culture to attract people back, not force them.
  • 气候变化紧迫；尽管经济低迷，我们必须现在投资，因为绿色产业是未来。
    Climate change is urgent; we must invest now despite downturns, as green industries are the future.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 15年换12所学校太多了；一半的次数就能学到同样的教训。
    Moving schools 12 times in 15 years was too much; half would have taught the same lessons.
  • 直升机式育儿培养出骄纵的孩子；挣扎和自力更生至关重要。
    Helicopter parenting breeds entitled kids; struggle and self-reliance are essential.
  • 创业团队不是家庭；它是一支有教练的团队，教练可以不带感情地雇佣和解雇。
    A startup team is not a family; it's a team with a coach who can hire and fire unemotionally.
  • 独自创始人很孤独；拥有互补技能的联合创始人对成功至关重要。
    Sole founders are lonely; a co-founder with complementary skills is critical for success.
  • 四天工作周和反奋斗文化是错误的；顶尖人才工作非常努力。
    The four-day work week and anti-hustle culture are misguided; top performers work intensely.
  • 经济衰退是自然的钟摆摆动；在别人恐惧时要贪婪，而非同情。
    Downturns are natural pendulum swings; be greedy when others are scared, not sympathetic.
