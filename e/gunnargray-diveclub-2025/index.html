在 AI 时代作为通才设计师茁壮成长

Thriving as a Generalist Designer in the Age of AI

本期速览 · Overview

Perplexity 移动设计负责人 Gunnar Gray 分享如何通过保持好奇心、在构建中学习以及在快节奏的 AI 产品环境中产生影响，作为通才设计师茁壮成长。

Gunnar Gray, mobile design lead at Perplexity, shares how to thrive as a generalist designer by embracing curiosity, learning through building, and making an impact in a fast-paced AI product environment.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 通才设计师在 AI 初创公司中通过横跨产品、品牌和代码而茁壮成长。
    Generalist designers thrive in AI startups by spanning product, brand, and code.
  • 用代码原型比静态工具更快地加速学习和迭代。
    Prototyping in code accelerates learning and iteration over static tools.
  • 语音界面需要设计系统状态和反馈，而不仅仅是像素。
    Voice interfaces demand designing system states and feedback, not just pixels.

核心观点 · Key points

  • AI产品是一种新媒介，需要以结果为导向、上下文感知和多模态设计。
    AI products are a new medium requiring outcome-driven, context-aware, and multimodal design.
  • 设计师应通过代码原型来加速学习和迭代周期。
    Designers should prototype in code to speed up learning and iteration cycles.
  • 好奇心会累积；学习新工具如着色器或编程会打开新机会。
    Curiosity compounds; learning new tools like shaders or coding opens new opportunities.
  • 通才设计师在初创公司中通过跨产品、品牌和代码的贡献而蓬勃发展。
    Generalist designers thrive at startups by contributing across product, brand, and code.
  • 语音界面需要设计系统状态和反馈循环，而不仅仅是像素。
    Voice interfaces require designing system states and feedback loops, not just pixels.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 设计师应该用代码构建，而不是在Figma等静态工具中花费太长时间。
    Designers should build in code rather than spend too long in static tools like Figma.
  • 由于延迟问题，按键通话按钮可以让语音比免提感觉更快。
    Push-to-talk buttons can make voice feel faster than hands-free due to latency issues.
  • 产品中的品牌是以非显而易见的方式兑现承诺，而不仅仅是视觉识别。
    Brand in product is about keeping promises in non-obvious ways, not just visual identity.
  • 设计师应该每年通过学习新技能（如编程或3D）来重塑自己。
    Designers should reinvent themselves yearly by learning a new skill like coding or 3D.
  • AI降低了非技术设计师编程的门槛，使其成为必备工具。
    AI lowers the barrier for non-technical designers to code, making it a must-learn tool.
