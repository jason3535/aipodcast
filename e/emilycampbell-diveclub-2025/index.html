本期速览 · Overview

HackerRank 设计副总裁 Emily Campbell 分享了一个设计 AI 交互的框架，从设计师在循环中转变为人类在循环中。

Emily Campbell, VP of Design at HackerRank, shares a framework for designing AI interactions, shifting from designer-in-the-loop to human-in-the-loop.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 设计师现在要促进人与 AI 的关系，而不仅仅是构建界面。
    Designers now facilitate human-AI relationships, not just build interfaces.
  • AI 交互遵循信任建立循环：意图、提交、验证、迭代。
    AI interactions follow a trust-building loop: intent, submission, verification, iteration.
  • 引导是持续的；AI 在学习用户时会展现新能力。
    Onboarding is continuous; AI reveals new capabilities as it learns the user.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 设计师现在要促进人与AI的关系，而不仅仅是构建界面。
    Designers now facilitate human-AI relationships, not just build interfaces.
  • AI交互遵循信任建立循环：意图、提交、验证、迭代。
    AI interactions follow a trust-building loop: intent, submission, verification, iteration.
  • 上手是持续的过程；AI随着了解用户而展现新能力。
    Onboarding is continuous; AI reveals new capabilities as it learns the user.
  • 诸如寻路器、调谐器和调节器等模式有助于构建AI用户体验设计。
    Patterns like wayfinders, tuners, and governors help structure AI UX design.
  • 通过展示工作、规划模式和清晰的AI推理来建立信任。
    Trust is built by showing work, planning modes, and legible AI reasoning.
  • 设计师必须理解模型行为，而不仅仅是视觉UI，才能打造体验。
    Designers must understand model behavior, not just visual UI, to craft experiences.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • AI设计应模仿人类雇佣：从小处开始，验证，再授予自主权。
    AI design should mimic human hiring: start small, verify, then grant autonomy.
  • 聊天界面是临时支架；更深层的交互会变成基于上下文的。
    The chat interface is a temporary scaffold; deeper interaction becomes context-based.
  • 提示增强器消除了用户成为提示专家的需求。
    Prompt enhancers remove the need for users to be prompt experts.
  • 像“展示你的工作”这样的信任建立模式对AI比以往任何时候都更重要。
    Trust-building patterns like 'show your work' are more critical than ever for AI.
  • AI中的同意往往很差；设计师必须解决数据收集的伦理问题。
    Consent in AI is often poor; designers must address data collection ethics.
  • 设计师应快速用代码原型化，以理解AI在体验中的作用。
    Designers should prototype in code quickly to understand AI's role in experience.
