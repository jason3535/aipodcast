本期速览 · Overview

OpenAI Codex 产品与工程负责人 Andrew Ambrosino 探讨 AI 如何颠覆产品开发流程，让实现变得廉价，品味成为新瓶颈。

Andrew Ambrosino, product and engineering lead for Codex at OpenAI, discusses how AI is inverting the product development process, making implementation cheap and taste the new bottleneck.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 当实现变得廉价时，策展和品味成为新的瓶颈。
    Curation and taste are the new bottlenecks as implementation becomes cheap.
  • 产品团队应从自上而下的规划转向自下而上的探索。
    Product teams should shift from top-down planning to bottoms-up exploration.
  • AI 在设计上表现不佳，因为难以评判品味且缺乏新颖性。
    AI struggles with design due to difficulty in grading taste and lack of novelty.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 实现不再昂贵；策展和品味成为新的瓶颈。
    Implementation is no longer expensive; curation and taste are the new bottlenecks.
  • 产品团队必须从自上而下的规划转向由品味驱动的自下而上探索。
    Product teams must shift from top-down planning to bottoms-up exploration with taste-driven curation.
  • AI 在设计上仍然糟糕，因为品味难以评分且缺乏新颖性。
    AI is still bad at design due to difficulty in grading taste and lack of novelty.
  • 设计流程并未消亡而是转型：原型和生产代码现在充当设计产物。
    The design process is not dead but transformed: prototypes and production code now serve as design artifacts.
  • 角色融合正在发生，但专业性和最佳实践仍然重要。
    Role collapse is happening, but specialties and best practices remain important.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • PRD 并未消亡；媒介必须匹配阶段：文档用于清晰，原型用于测试。
    PRDs are not dead; the medium must match the stage: documents for clarity, prototypes for testing.
  • 同一产品形态可能仅因模型时机而非功能而失败或成功。
    The same product shape can fail or succeed based solely on model timing, not features.
  • Codex 虽为开发者设计，却作为通用知识工作工具成功。
    Codex succeeded as a general knowledge work tool despite being designed for developers.
  • AI 模型增加代码复杂性；我们需要更擅长删除代码的模型。
    AI models increase code complexity; we need models that are better at deleting code.
  • 最好的桌面应用是连接其他工具的大本营，而非单一超级应用。
    The best desktop app is a home base that connects to other tools, not a monolithic super app.
