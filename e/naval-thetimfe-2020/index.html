本期速览 · Overview

Tim Ferriss 采访 AngelList 联合创始人、Twitter 和 Uber 等公司的天使投资人 Naval Ravikant，探讨他对财富、幸福和工作未来的哲学。

Tim Ferriss interviews Naval Ravikant, co-founder of AngelList and angel investor in Twitter, Uber, and more, discussing his philosophy on wealth, happiness, and the future of work.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 财富需要拥有股权，而不是出租时间。
    Wealth requires owning equity, not renting time.
  • 与可信赖的人长期思考能让一切复利增长。
    Long-term thinking with trustworthy people compounds everything.
  • 自我审视和冥想能减少焦虑、改善生活。
    Self-examination and meditation reduce anxiety and improve life.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 财富需要拥有企业股权，而不是出租你的时间。
    Wealth requires owning equity in a business, not renting out your time.
  • 长期思维是所有有效自助和成功的基础。
    Long-term thinking is the foundation of all effective self-help and success.
  • 最重要的关系是与自己的关系；自我审视能减少焦虑。
    The most important relationship is with yourself; self-examination reduces anxiety.
  • 加密货币实现了无需中介的主权抵抗型财富存储和转移。
    Cryptocurrencies enable sovereign-resistant wealth storage and transfer without intermediaries.
  • 理解第一原理胜过记忆术语；真正的知识是可以重新推导的。
    Understanding first principles beats memorizing jargon; true knowledge is rederivable.
  • 选择长期游戏和长期伙伴，在所有领域获得复利回报。
    Choose long-term games with long-term people for compound returns in all areas.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 科学不是共识或投票；它需要可证伪性和风险预测。
    Science is not consensus or voting; it requires falsifiability and risky predictions.
  • 稳定币有隐藏成本：欺诈风险、审查风险或崩盘风险。
    Stable coins have hidden costs: fraud risk, censorship risk, or blow-up risk.
  • 最好的关系不觉得是工作；毫不费力的化学反应胜过刻意努力。
    The best relationships don't feel like work; effortless chemistry beats effort.
  • 赢得游戏的理由是为了摆脱它，而不是继续玩下去。
    The reason to win the game is to be free of it, not to keep playing.
  • 冷静是一种超能力；焦虑并非高效的必要条件。
    Calmness is a superpower; anxiety is not necessary for effectiveness.
  • 冥想应该摧毁你当前的生活；它带来真正的改变，而非舒适。
    Meditation should ruin your current life; it leads to real change, not comfort.
