本期速览 · Overview

OpenAI 产品设计负责人 Ian Silber 分享团队如何工作、以 AI 为材料进行设计以及设计师角色的演变。

OpenAI's head of product design Ian Silber shares how the team works, designs with AI, and evolves the designer role.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 设计师必须与模型协作，而不仅仅是像素，才能将 AI 产品化。
    Designers must work with the model, not just pixels, to productize AI.
  • 系统思维和基于实时模型的快速原型设计是关键。
    Systems thinking and rapid prototyping with live models are key.
  • 设计师的角色演变为编辑和策展人，而非消失。
    The designer's role evolves into an editor and curator, not disappearing.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 设计师必须与模型紧密合作，理解其能力和局限，将其产品化。
    Designers must work closely with the model, understanding its capabilities and limitations to productize it.
  • 模型本身是核心产品；设计应利用 token 和对话而非像素。
    The model itself is the core product; design should leverage tokens and conversation over pixels.
  • 系统思维至关重要：设计人类和模型都能推理的原始构件。
    Systems thinking is crucial: design primitives that both humans and models can reason about.
  • 使用实时模型快速原型设计是关键；设计师应构建功能原型，而不仅仅是模型。
    Rapid prototyping with live models is key; designers should build functional prototypes, not just mockups.
  • 设计师的角色正在演变为编辑和策展人，而非消失。
    The role of the designer is evolving into an editor and curator, not disappearing.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • OpenAI 并非领先数年；他们紧跟进展，边做边学。
    OpenAI is not years ahead; they run closely with advancements, figuring things out as they go.
  • 设计师应减少在 Figma 上的时间，更多关注模型行为和系统提示。
    Designers should spend less time in Figma and more on model behavior and system prompts.
  • 引导流程可完全由模型完成，取代传统的静态流程。
    Onboarding can be done entirely by the model, replacing traditional static flows.
  • 能力差距意味着模型能做的比当前产品展示的更多；设计必须弥合这一差距。
    The capability gap means models can do more than current products expose; design must bridge that.
  • 优秀的设计师现在需要推动技术前进，而不仅仅是响应需求。
    Great designers now need to push technology forward, not just react to user needs.
