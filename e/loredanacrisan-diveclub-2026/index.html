本期速览 · Overview

Figma 首席设计官分享她作为古典钢琴家和音响工程师的背景如何塑造她对 AI 工具的看法，敦促设计师将心灵和灵魂注入作品。

Figma's Chief Design Officer shares how her background as a classical pianist and sound engineer shapes her philosophy on AI tools, urging designers to bring heart and soul into their work.

要点 · TL;DR

  • AI 是工具，人类的灵魂和品味定义优秀设计。
    AI is a tool; human soul and taste define great design.
  • 品味是努力和用心，而非天赋。
    Taste is hard work and care, not an innate gift.
  • 设计师应创建系统而非屏幕，用 AI 实现精准控制。
    Designers should create systems, not just screens, using AI for precision.

核心观点 · Key points

  • AI 是工具，人类必须为输出注入灵魂和心意。
    AI is a tool; the human must bring the soul and heart to the output.
  • 品味等同于用心和刻意，通过努力和热情培养。
    Taste equals care and intentionality, developed through hard work and passion.
  • 设计师应创建系统而非仅屏幕，利用 AI 实现精确和控制。
    Designers should create systems, not just screens, using AI for precision and control.
  • AI 提升了脱颖而出的需求；真实性变得更有价值。
    AI elevates the need to stand out; authenticity becomes more valuable.
  • 评审是瓶颈；在画布上可视化想法能促进更好的协作。
    Review is a bottleneck; visualizing ideas on canvas enables better collaboration.
  • 设计师必须拥抱评估，以在非确定性 AI 系统中定义质量。
    Designers must embrace evals to define quality in non-deterministic AI systems.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 品味并非天生；它是努力和用心，而非神奇天赋。
    Taste is not innate; it's hard work and care, not a magical gift.
  • 无人参与的 AI 生成内容显得不真实，使用户疏离。
    AI-generated content without human input feels inauthentic and disengages users.
  • 设计师应提交代码并修复 bug，模糊与工程师的界限。
    Designers should ship code and fix bugs, blurring roles with engineers.
  • AI 应搭建工作空间，但人类必须调整并使其个性化。
    AI should set up the workspace, but humans must tweak and make it their own.
  • 最重要的设计决策关乎算法，而非按钮。
    The most important design decisions are about algorithms, not buttons.
  • 设计师需要熟悉评估和代码等技术领域。
    Designers need to be comfortable with technical areas like evals and code.
