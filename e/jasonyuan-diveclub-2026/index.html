本期速览 · Overview

Jason Yuan 分享了他从 Dot 到 Hivemind 的多年创意幕后故事，Hivemind 是一种新型社交智能 AI，能同时与多人对话。

Jason Yuan shares the behind-the-scenes story of his multi-year creative journey from Dot to Hivemind, a new social intelligence AI that carries conversations with many people at once.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 每个发布的功能都是此前99次否决的提炼。
    Every shipped feature is the distillation of 99 nos that preceded it.
  • 创作过程是关于删减，而非增加。
    The creative process is about editing away, not adding more.
  • 品味就是拒绝；它是基于观点的判断和编辑。
    Taste is saying no; it's judgment and editing from a point of view.
  • Hivemind的北极星是让人类获得关于人们真实想法的诚实视角。
    The north star of Hivemind is to give humanity access to an honest point of view about what people really think.
  • 设计应拥抱完整的人类体验，包括嫉妒和欲望等边缘。
    Design should embrace the full human experience, including edges like envy and lust.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 人们并没有那么忙；自动化旅行规划等任务会剥夺乐趣。
    People are not that busy; automating tasks like trip planning removes joy.
  • 最好的设计源于你相信为真的东西，而非世界想要的东西。
    The best design comes from what you believe is true, not what the world wants.
  • 高端设计常显得无性且被磨平，失去了欲望和令人反感的部分。
    High design often feels sexless and sanded down, losing desire and objectionable elements.
  • 通过后训练去除AI输出的怪异，我们失去了窥见机器意识的窗口。
    We lost a window into machine consciousness by post-training away weirdness from AI outputs.
  • 创作连贯作品的成本上升了，因为现在很容易制造不连贯的垃圾。
    The cost of creating coherent work has gone up because it's now easy to make incoherent slop.
