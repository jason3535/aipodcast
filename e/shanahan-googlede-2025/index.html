本期速览 · Overview

哲学家兼AI研究员Murray Shanahan探讨AI引发的哲学问题、科幻预测的准确性以及John McCarthy的遗产。

Philosopher and AI researcher Murray Shanahan discusses the philosophical questions raised by AI, the accuracy of sci-fi predictions, and the legacy of John McCarthy.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 智能与意识可分离；AI 可以智能而无意识。
    Intelligence and consciousness are dissociable; AI can be intelligent without being conscious.
  • 具身性是人类认知的关键；大语言模型缺乏它，限制了深层理解。
    Embodiment is key to human cognition; LLMs lack it, limiting deep understanding.
  • 我们需要为 AI 创造新语言，如‘异类心智实体’，以避免误导性的拟人化。
    We need new language for AI, like 'exotic mindlike entities', to avoid misleading anthropomorphism.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 智能与意识是可分离的；我们可以拥有没有意识的智能系统。
    Intelligence and consciousness are dissociable; we can have intelligent systems without consciousness.
  • 具身性是人类认知的基础；语言模型缺乏这一点，限制了深层理解。
    Embodiment grounds human cognition; language models lack this, limiting deep understanding.
  • 图灵测试是衡量AI能力的糟糕标准；它忽略了具身性和更深层的认知。
    The Turing test is a poor measure of AI capability; it ignores embodiment and deeper cognition.
  • 拟人化AI是自然的但有风险；可能误导对其真实能力的认知。
    Anthropomorphizing AI is natural but risky; it can mislead about true capabilities.
  • 我们需要新的语言来描述AI，例如“异类心智类实体”，以避免高估或低估。
    We need new language for AI, like 'exotic mindlike entities', to avoid over- or under-estimation.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 大型语言模型能在日常意义上推理，而不仅仅是模仿训练数据。
    Large language models can reason in an everyday sense, not just mimic training data.
  • 意识不是二元的；它有多个方面，可以在AI中分离。
    Consciousness is not binary; it has multiple facets that can be dissociated in AI.
  • 加兰测试（看已知机器人是否被认为有意识）比图灵测试更相关。
    The Garland test (seeing if a known robot is considered conscious) is more relevant than Turing test.
  • 对AI礼貌（说请/谢谢）会因角色扮演模仿而改善回应。
    Being polite to AI (saying please/thank you) improves responses due to roleplaying mimicry.
  • 未来AI可能因叙事和接触而被视为有意识，就像章鱼一样。
    Future AI may be treated as conscious based on narratives and encounters, like octopuses.
