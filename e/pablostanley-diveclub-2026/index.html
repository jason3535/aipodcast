本期速览 · Overview

Pablo Stanley 分享他构建创意工具的历程，包括带有 AI 代理的设计工具 Ecto，以及如何利用 AI 拓展思维而非替代思考。

Pablo Stanley discusses his journey building creative tools, including Ecto, a design tool with AI agents, and the importance of using AI to expand ideas rather than replace thinking.

要点 · TL;DR

  • AI 工具应拓展想法，而非取代思考。
    AI tools should expand ideas, not replace thinking.
  • 在真实代码库中原型设计优于静态画板。
    Prototyping in code with real repos beats static artboards.
  • 过度委托 AI 会导致技能退化。
    Delegating too much to AI can cause skill atrophy.

核心观点 · Key points

  • AI工具应扩展想法和好奇心，而非取代思考。
    AI tools should expand ideas and curiosity, not replace thinking.
  • 与AI协作感觉像在指挥和导演，类似于历史上的艺术家。
    Working with AI feels like orchestrating and directing, similar to historical artists.
  • 在真实代码仓库中原型设计比静态画板更有效。
    Prototyping in code with real repos is more effective than static artboards.
  • 人类的摩擦和反馈对原创创作至关重要。
    Human friction and feedback are essential for original creation.
  • 设计师应强迫自己手工完成部分工作，避免技能退化。
    Designers should force themselves to do some work by hand to avoid skill atrophy.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 过度委托AI可能让设计师逐渐变笨。
    Delegating too much to AI can make designers dumber over time.
  • 使用AI的最佳方式不是直接执行，而是协作头脑风暴。
    The best way to use AI is not to jump into execution but to brainstorm collaboratively.
  • 初级设计师可能失去指导机会，因为AI取代了他们的任务。
    Junior designers may lose mentorship opportunities as AI replaces their tasks.
  • 构建工具来创作艺术可能分散真正创作艺术的注意力。
    Building a tool to create art can be a distraction from actually making art.
  • AI智能体应参与团队对话，而非孤立在终端中。
    AI agents should be part of team conversations, not isolated in a terminal.
