本期速览 · Overview

Stripe 设计主管 Katie Dill 探讨设计在 Stripe 的演变，从全新 Stripe.com 的设计决策到他们寻找设计师的特质。

Stripe's Head of Design Katie Dill discusses how design is evolving at Stripe, from the new Stripe.com design decisions to the traits they look for in designers.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 设计师应追求 15/10 的体验，在 AI 提升下限的基础上拔高上限。
    Designers should aim for 15/10 experiences, raising the ceiling beyond AI's floor.
  • AI 支持快速原型，让设计师探索更多想法并更快迭代。
    AI enables rapid prototyping, letting designers explore more ideas and iterate faster.
  • 高标准和坦诚反馈对 Stripe 的设计质量至关重要。
    High standards and candid feedback are crucial for quality design at Stripe.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 设计师应专注于提升上限，而非仅提高下限，打造15/10的体验。
    Designers should focus on raising the ceiling, not just the floor, by creating 15/10 experiences.
  • AI支持快速原型制作，让设计师能探索更多想法并更快迭代。
    AI enables rapid prototyping, allowing designers to explore more ideas and iterate faster.
  • 高标准和坦诚反馈的文化对Stripe的高质量工作至关重要。
    High standards and a culture of candid feedback are crucial for quality work at Stripe.
  • 好奇心、主动性和对AI的真正兴趣是新设计招聘的关键特质。
    Curiosity, agency, and genuine interest in AI are key traits for new design hires.
  • 设计师应通过视觉而非文字优先考虑用户体验，用图像传达信息。
    Designers should prioritize user experience by showing rather than telling, using visuals to communicate.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 为美而美具有实用性；它带来愉悦并传达关怀，即使不具功能性。
    Beauty for beauty's sake has utility; it brings joy and communicates care, even if not functional.
  • 现代设计可能过于冰冷；我们需要在不杂乱的前提下重新引入人性和愉悦。
    Modern design can be too sterile; we need to reintroduce humanity and delight without clutter.
  • AI应用于放慢节奏并增加关怀，而不仅仅是加速和产出更多。
    AI should be used to slow down and add care, not just to speed up and produce more.
  • 设计师应自己构建原型而非推销想法，跳过演示文稿和会议。
    Designers should build prototypes themselves rather than pitch ideas, skipping decks and meetings.
  • 现状UI模式可能限制魔力；有时我们需要为更好的用户体验重新发明。
    Status quo UI patterns can limit magic; sometimes we need to reinvent for better user experience.
