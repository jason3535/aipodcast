为 AI 的现在和未来而设计

Designing for AI's Present and Future

本期速览 · Overview

Anthropic 产品设计负责人 Joel Lewenstein 探讨设计 Claude 等前沿 AI 产品的独特挑战，平衡即时用户价值与长期根本性变革。

Joel Lewenstein, Head of Product Design at Anthropic, discusses the unique challenges of designing cutting-edge AI products like Claude, balancing immediate user value with long-term radical change.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 语言作为界面是深刻且持久的，不仅仅是聊天机器人阶段。
    Language as interface is profound and lasting, not just a chatbot phase.
  • 如果假设明确，用“寻找问题的解决方案”进行原型设计是有效的。
    Prototyping with 'solutions in search of a problem' is valid if assumptions are clear.
  • 心理安全感使设计师能够在 AI 团队中承担创造性风险。
    Psychological safety enables designers to take creative risks in AI teams.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 设计师必须掌握提示工程，将其作为AI产品设计过程的一部分。
    Designers must master prompting as part of the design process for AI products.
  • 语言作为界面具有深远意义，即使UI超越聊天机器人，它仍将是核心。
    Language as an interface is profound and will remain central even as UIs evolve beyond chatbots.
  • 在假设明确的前提下，原型设计和“寻找问题解决方案”是有效的。
    Prototyping and 'solutions in search of a problem' are valid when assumptions are clear.
  • AI产品需要平衡降低新手门槛和提高专家上限。
    AI products need to balance lowering the floor for novices and raising the ceiling for experts.
  • 心理安全感对于设计师在AI产品团队中承担创意风险至关重要。
    Psychological safety is essential for designers to take creative risks in AI product teams.
  • 设计师应通过寻找能成为未来功能构建块的解决方案来贡献战略。
    Designers should contribute to strategy by finding solutions that become building blocks for future features.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 聊天机器人并非过渡阶段；语言作为界面具有深远而持久的意义。
    Chatbots are not just a phase; language as an interface is deeply profound and lasting.
  • 寻找问题解决方案并非天生不好；它们可以孕育新概念。
    Solutions in search of a problem are not inherently bad; they can seed new concepts.
  • 即使模型停止改进，仍有数年的设计工作来解锁当前能力。
    Even if models stopped improving, years of design work remain to unlock current capabilities.
  • AI的社会和情感功能可能与功能性生产力提升同样重要。
    AI's social and emotional jobs may be as important as functional productivity gains.
  • 提前向候选人提供面试评分标准可改善评估和候选人体验。
    Giving candidates the interview rubric upfront improves evaluation and candidate experience.
  • 组织设计是一个设计问题；将人与问题匹配比制定方案更关键。
    Org design is a design problem; matching people to problems is more critical than plays.
