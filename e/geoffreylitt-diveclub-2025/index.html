LLM 与可塑软件的未来

LLMs and the Future of Malleable Software

本期速览 · Overview

设计工程师 Jeffrey Lit 探讨 LLM 如何推动数字产品设计向个性化、用户可控的软件转变，超越传统 SaaS 模式。

Design engineer Jeffrey Lit discusses how LLMs are shifting digital product design towards personalized, user-controlled software, moving beyond traditional SaaS models.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 可塑软件将控制权从开发者转移到用户，支持本地定制。
    Malleable software shifts control from developers to users, enabling local customization.
  • 版本控制是人机协作创意工作的基础。
    Version control is essential for human-AI collaboration in creative work.
  • AI 应教导并培养批判性思维，而不仅仅是自动化任务。
    AI should teach and foster critical thinking, not just automate tasks.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 可塑软件让用户能根据本地情境自定义工具，将控制权从开发者转移到用户。
    Malleable software empowers users to customize tools for their local context, shifting control from developers to users.
  • 在设计中暴露系统结构（如自行车上的数字齿轮）能让用户更深入理解并发挥创造力。
    Exposing systemic structure in design, like numbered gears on a bike, enables deeper user understanding and creativity.
  • 版本控制是人机协作的基础，允许用户对AI输出进行分支、审查和合并。
    Version control is fundamental for human-AI collaboration, allowing users to branch, review, and merge AI outputs.
  • AI聊天是高级用户界面，其优势在于灵活性，但对新手缺乏引导。
    AI chat is a power user interface; its strength lies in flexibility, but it lacks onboarding for novices.
  • 设计师的角色正从创造成品转向设计模式和工具包供用户使用。
    The role of designers is shifting from creating finished products to designing patterns and toolkits for users.
  • AI应被用于学习和保持对细节的掌控，而不仅仅是自动化和委派任务。
    AI should be used to learn and stay connected to details, not just to automate and delegate tasks.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 可塑软件反而带来更稳定、可预测的环境，因为用户能控制变化。
    Malleable software leads to more stable, predictable environments because users have control over changes.
  • 好的默认设置只解决了70%的问题，其余需要AI教会用户如何自定义。
    Good defaults are only 70% of the solution; the rest requires AI to teach users how to customize.
  • AI工具应优先考虑教学和学习，而不仅仅是速度；摩擦反而能推动更好的创意成果。
    AI tools should prioritize teaching and learning, not just speed; friction can drive better creative outcomes.
  • 当前应用商店模式是分享自定义工具的瓶颈；分享应像发送链接一样简单。
    The current app store model is a bottleneck for sharing custom tools; sharing should be as easy as a link.
  • 像外科医生一样编码意味着在AI辅助下保持亲自动手，而不是成为智能体的管理者。
    Coding like a surgeon means staying hands-on with AI assistance, not becoming a manager of agents.
  • AI可以通过解释其工作来帮助用户成为更批判性的思考者，而不仅仅是替他们完成工作。
    AI can help users become more critical thinkers by explaining its work, not just doing it for them.
