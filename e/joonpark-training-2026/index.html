本期速览 · Overview

Simile 创始人 June 探讨了生成式智能体在小镇实验中模拟涌现社会行为的过程，以及利用模拟指导社会的愿景。

June, founder of Simile, discusses how generative agents in Smallville simulated emergent social behaviors, and the vision for using simulations to guide society.

要点 · TL;DR

  • LLM 能以 85% 的准确率模拟人类行为，但需要真实世界数据弥合言行差距。
    LLMs can simulate human behavior with 85% accuracy, but need real-world data to close the say-do gap.
  • 模拟能预测决策的二级影响，超越单问题调查。
    Simulations can predict second-order effects of decisions, beyond single-question surveys.
  • 最终目标是打造类似 CERN 的人类社会模拟器，解锁社会科学。
    The ultimate goal is a CERN-like simulator for human society to unlock social science.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 大语言模型能从训练数据中编码人类行为，实现逼真的智能体模拟。
    LLMs can encode human behavior from training data, enabling realistic agent simulations.
  • 模拟预测人类行为的准确率可达人们自我复现的 85%。
    Simulations can predict human behavior 85% as accurately as people replicate themselves.
  • 当前模型在模拟人类多样性上遇到瓶颈；下一个前沿是建模主观价值观。
    Current models plateau in simulating human diversity; next frontier is modeling subjective values.
  • 需要真实世界的行为数据来弥合模拟中的言行差距。
    Real-world behavioral data is needed to close the say-do gap in simulations.
  • 模拟能测试决策的二级影响，超越单一问题调查。
    Simulations enable testing second-order impacts of decisions, beyond single-question surveys.
  • 最终愿景是建造类似 CERN 的人类社会模拟器，解锁社会科学。
    The ultimate vision is a CERN-like simulator for human society, unlocking social science.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 为理性优化的前沿模型并不最适合模拟非理性的人类行为。
    Frontier models optimized for rationality are not best for simulating irrational human behavior.
  • 模拟可能比在线实验更具代表性，后者偏向特定群体。
    Simulations can be more representative than online experiments, which skew to certain groups.
  • 某些模拟即使有误差也会收敛；发散则通过置信区间处理。
    Some simulations converge despite errors; divergence is handled via confidence intervals.
  • 耗资一亿美元的模拟运行可能解决根本性的社会问题。
    Simulating a 100-million-dollar run could solve fundamental societal questions.
  • 如今的智能体模型能复制完整的人类丰富性，而不仅是简单的红蓝点。
    Agent-based models today can replicate full human richness, not just simple red/blue dots.
  • 该领域需要新的统计标准（如 p<0.05）来验证模拟有效性。
    The field needs new statistical standards, like p<0.05, for simulation validity.
