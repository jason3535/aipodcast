构建 AI 产品：雕刻而非建造

Building AI Products: Carving vs. Building

本期速览 · Overview

Runway 联合创始人兼首席设计官 Alejandro 探讨了 AI 产品开发的独特性如何影响设计人才评估、技术技能的重要性，以及从早期生成式 AI 实验到设想全长生成电影的历程。

Alejandro, co-founder and chief design officer of Runway, discusses how the unique nature of AI product development influences design talent evaluation, the importance of technical skills, and the journey from early generative AI experiments to envisioning full-length generated films.

要点 · TL;DR

  • AI 产品设计需要技术技能来构建真实原型。
    AI product design requires technical skills to build real prototypes.
  • Runway 实现长视频叙事的愿景始终如一。
    Runway's vision of enabling long-form storytelling has been consistent.
  • 随着 AI 输出质量趋同，直觉将比品味更重要。
    Intuition will surpass taste as AI outputs become uniformly good.

核心观点 · Key points

  • AI 产品设计的未来需要技术能力来构建和测试真实原型。
    The future of product design in AI requires technical skills to build and test real prototypes.
  • 最佳成果来自设计师、创意人员、研究人员和工程师的协作。
    Best outcomes come when designers, creatives, researchers, and engineers collaborate together.
  • Runway 通过生成式 AI 实现长片叙事的愿景自始至终保持一致。
    Runway's vision of enabling long-form storytelling through generative AI has been consistent since the start.
  • 当 AI 输出普遍优质时，直觉将比品味更重要。
    Intuition will be more important than taste as AI outputs become uniformly good.
  • 民主化工具将使来自弱势地区的更多元故事成为可能。
    Democratized tools will enable more diverse stories from underrepresented regions.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 设计团队应让前端工程师轮岗到设计团队，以建立共同直觉。
    Design teams should embed frontend engineers in design rotations to build shared intuition.
  • Runway 没有产品路线图，而是采用研究探索领域。
    Runway has no product roadmap; instead it uses research areas of exploration.
  • 构建 AI 产品如同雕刻，而非遵循预定计划。
    Building AI products is like carving a sculpture, not following a predefined plan.
  • 设计师不应专精于某一领域，而应在产品各方面轮换。
    Designers should not specialize; they should rotate across all aspects of the product.
  • 最好的故事尚未被讲述，AI 工具将解锁它们。
    The best stories have not been told yet, and AI tools will unlock them.
