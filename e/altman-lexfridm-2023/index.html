本期速览 · Overview

Sam Altman 讨论了 AGI 早期受到的嘲笑、ChatGPT 的关键时刻，以及 RLHF 在让 AI 变得有用中的作用。

Sam Altman discusses the early mockery of AGI, the pivotal moment of ChatGPT, and the role of RLHF in making AI useful.

要点 · TL;DR

  • RLHF 用极少数据对齐模型，使其更可用。
    RLHF aligns models with little data, making them vastly more usable.
  • 早期公开部署有助于社会适应并发现缺陷。
    Early public deployment helps society adapt and discover flaws.
  • AI 应放大人类意志，而非被视为生物。
    AI should amplify human will, not be treated as a creature.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 基于人类反馈的强化学习（RLHF）用极少的数据就能让模型对齐人类偏好，大幅提升可用性。
    RLHF aligns models to human preferences with remarkably little data, making them vastly more usable.
  • 早期公开部署 AI 能让社会逐步适应，并帮助发现能力与缺陷。
    Deploying AI early and publicly allows society to adapt and helps discover both capabilities and flaws.
  • 对齐与能力紧密相关；更好的对齐通常带来更强的能力。
    Alignment and capability are closely linked; better alignment often leads to better capabilities.
  • 智能和能源的成本将大幅下降，从经济和政治上改变社会。
    The cost of intelligence and energy will dramatically fall, transforming society economically and politically.
  • AI 应是放大人类意志的工具，而非生物；将其视为生物是危险的。
    AI should be a tool that amplifies human will, not a creature; treating it as a creature is dangerous.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • GPT-4 并非 AGI（通用人工智能）；它缺乏真正通用智能的深度和广度。
    GPT-4 is not an AGI; it lacks the depth and generality of true general intelligence.
  • 近期最大的 AI 风险是虚假信息和经济冲击，而非超级智能的对齐问题。
    The biggest near-term AI risks are disinformation and economic shocks, not superintelligent alignment.
  • OpenAI 的利润上限结构对于抵制资本主义压力、优先考虑安全至关重要。
    OpenAI's capped-profit structure is crucial to resist capitalist pressures and prioritize safety.
  • AI 不会消除人类工作的需求；人们将找到新的创造性表达形式。
    AI will not eliminate the need for human work; people will find new forms of creative expression.
  • AI 带来的变化速度将超过制度适应能力，正如硅谷银行挤兑事件所示。
    The speed of change from AI will outpace institutional adaptation, as seen with the SVB bank run.
