从零设计 Perplexity 的 AI 浏览器 Comet

Designing Perplexity's AI Browser Comet from Scratch

埃莎·维拉 Escha Vera · Dive Club · 2025-09-26 · 约 58 分钟 · 原视频 ↗

打开互动全文版（中英对照 + 朗读 + 问答）→

本期速览 · Overview

Escha Vera 分享了作为设计师加入 Perplexity 并接手全新 AI 浏览器 Comet 的经历，从零开始，没有设计系统。

Escha Vera shares her journey of joining Perplexity as a designer and being handed the keys to the all-new AI browser Comet, starting without a design system.

要点 · TL;DR

  • Perplexity 的 Comet 浏览器从类似 Chrome 的熟悉设计入手，以降低 AI 采用门槛。
    Perplexity's Comet browser starts familiar like Chrome to ease AI adoption.
  • 用代码而非 Figma 构建设计系统，可实现 AI 产品的一致性和可扩展性。
    Design systems in code, not Figma, enable scalable AI product consistency.
  • AI 助手以标签页形式比窗口形式更有效。
    AI agents work better as tab-based assistants than window-based ones.

核心观点 · Key points

  • AI 工具应融入工作流，而非用作即时输出的老虎机。
    AI tools should be integrated into workflows, not used as slot machines for instant outputs.
  • 设计系统应存在于代码中而非仅 Figma，以实现跨产品的一致性和可扩展性。
    Design systems in code, not just Figma, enable consistency and scalability across products.
  • 新产品中的熟悉感可降低认知负荷，让用户专注于新颖的 AI 功能。
    Familiarity in new products reduces cognitive load, allowing users to focus on novel AI features.
  • 通过界面（滑块、下拉菜单）产品化 AI 比原始提示更适合普通用户。
    Productizing AI via interfaces (sliders, dropdowns) is better than raw prompts for lay users.
  • 设计团队的高度自主权和所有权可带来高影响力和更快的迭代。
    High autonomy and ownership in design teams lead to high impact and faster iteration.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • AI 浏览器应像 Chrome 一样熟悉，而非像 Arc 那样激进，以吸引主流用户。
    AI browser should start familiar like Chrome, not radically different like Arc, to attract mainstream users.
  • 基于智能体的浏览器助手采用标签页式而非窗口式更佳，与初始直觉相反。
    Agent-based browser assistant works better as tab-based than window-based, contrary to initial instinct.
  • 设计系统可在几周内从零构建，即使面对重大产品发布。
    Design systems can be built from scratch in a few weeks, even for a major product launch.
  • 使用 Suno 等 AI 工具生成音乐被低估；它们让非音乐人也能创作音频。
    Using AI tools like Suno for music generation is underrated; they enable non-musicians to create audio.
  • 邀请制发布结合生成式艺术可营造专属感和兴奋感，尽管更复杂。
    Invite-only rollout with generative art creates exclusivity and excitement, despite being more complex.
  • AI 并非天生是老虎机；通过迭代混音，它可以成为创意跳板。
    AI is not inherently a slot machine; it can be a creative springboard through iterative remixing.
