本期速览 · Overview

ElevenLabs 设计主管 Ammaar Reshi 探讨 AI 原生产品设计的挑战与机遇，分享他从早期 AI 实验到利用 Midjourney 和 ChatGPT 创作爆款儿童绘本的经历。

Ammaar Reshi, Head of Design at ElevenLabs, discusses the challenges and opportunities of designing AI-native products, sharing his journey from early AI experiments to creating a viral kids' book using Midjourney and ChatGPT.

要点 · TL;DR

  • AI 设计应捕捉魔力，而不给用户增加复杂控制负担。
    AI design should capture magic without burdening users with complex controls.
  • 语音作为界面将在社会规范转变后成为主流，可能由苹果引领。
    Voice as an interface will go mainstream once social norms shift, likely led by Apple.
  • 设计师应贴近业务和工程以建立信誉和影响力。
    Designers should get close to business and engineering to build credibility and influence.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 用AI设计应捕捉魔力，而不让用户承受复杂控制的负担。
    Designing with AI should capture magic without burdening users with complex controls.
  • AI通过直接理解用户意图，能够重写旧的UI模式。
    AI enables rewriting old UI patterns by understanding user intent directly.
  • 一旦社会规范转变，语音界面将成为主流，可能由苹果引领。
    Voice as an interface will become mainstream once social norms shift, likely led by Apple.
  • 设计师应靠近业务和工程，以建立信誉和影响力。
    Designers should get close to the business and engineering to build credibility and influence.
  • 实验和黑客文化是构建创新AI产品的核心。
    A culture of experimentation and hacking is core to building innovative AI products.
  • 异步视频分享帮助团队对齐，并使设计师成为更好的故事讲述者。
    Async video sharing helps teams align and makes designers better storytellers.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 带有文本字段和生成按钮的提示框可能是最简单的设计。
    The prompt box with text field and generate button may be the best design for simplicity.
  • 设计师不应总是提前与大量用户交谈；直觉可能更有价值。
    Designers should not always talk to many users upfront; intuition can be more valuable.
  • 在早期初创公司，没有不可逆的决定；高信任度支持快速实验。
    In early-stage startups, no decision is irreversible; high trust enables rapid experimentation.
  • 目标不是用AI一次性完成；完善和调整提示至关重要。
    The goal is not to one-shot with AI; refining and massaging prompts is essential.
  • 设计师不应回避编码；这能赢得工程团队的尊重。
    Designers should not shy away from coding; it builds respect with engineering teams.
  • 分享原始、未过滤的提示和失败比精心制作的课程更有价值。
    Sharing raw, unfiltered prompts and failures is more valuable than polished courses.
