本期速览 · Overview

2025 年，基于可验证奖励的强化学习让语言模型在数学和竞赛编程中达到专家水平，预计年底前软件工程智能体可完成一天的工作量。

In 2025, RL with verifiable rewards has enabled language models to achieve expert-level performance in math and competitive programming, with software engineering agents expected to handle a day's work by year-end.
