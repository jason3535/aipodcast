核心观点 · Key points

  • AI 现在具有智能体能力，能做有用工作并为此付费，而不仅仅是生成文本。
    AI is now agentic, doing useful work you can pay for, not just generating text.
  • 未来互联网将由数十亿 AI 智能体全天候主导，不断生成智能。
    Future internet will be dominated by billions of AI agents working 24/7, generating intelligence.
  • AI 产业是五层蛋糕：能源、芯片、基础设施、模型和应用。
    AI industry is a five-layer cake: energy, chips, infrastructure, models, and applications.
  • 不要害怕 AI；要害怕输给使用 AI 的人。把它当作超能力来使用。
    Don't fear AI; fear losing your job to someone who uses AI. Engage it as a superpower.
  • AI 通过自动化任务提升工作，而非消除目的。放射科医生和程序员比以往更紧缺。
    AI elevates jobs by automating tasks, not eliminating purpose. Radiologists and coders are more needed than ever.
  • AI 消除了技术鸿沟：现在每个人都能用自然语言编程。
    AI closes the technology divide: everyone can now program a computer using natural language.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 声称AI可能终结人类或神秘莫测是无稽之谈；它只是软件。
    Claims that AI might end humanity or is mysterious are nonsense; it's just software.
  • 放射学曾被预测会被AI消灭，但放射科医生的需求反而增加了。
    Radiology was predicted to be wiped out by AI, but demand for radiologists increased.
  • AI缩小了技术鸿沟；现在每个人都能编程，而不仅仅是懂C++的2%。
    AI closes the technology divide; now everyone can program computers, not just 2% with C++.
  • AI会摧毁工作的叙事是错误的；它创造了更多工作并提升了技艺。
    The narrative that AI will destroy jobs is wrong; it creates more jobs and elevates crafts.
  • 未来的互联网将由数十亿智能体使用，而不仅仅是人类，持续生成智能。
    Future internet will be used by billions of agents, not just humans, generating intelligence constantly.
  • AI工厂生产智能代币，类似于发电机生产电力。
    AI factories produce intelligence tokens, similar to how dynamos produce electricity.
