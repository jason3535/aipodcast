本期速览 · Overview

一位科技投资者探讨了平衡生活、拥抱多元兴趣的重要性，以及持续学习和重新开始的乐趣。

A tech investor discusses the importance of a balanced life, embracing multiple interests, and the joy of continuous learning and starting over.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 幸福是一种选择；欲望是一份让你在得到之前一直不快乐的契约。
    Happiness is a choice; desire is a contract to be unhappy until you get what you want.
  • 致富要靠拥有企业的一部分；出租时间无法让你变富。
    To get rich, own a piece of a business; renting your time won't make you wealthy.
  • 现代人的挣扎在于抵抗社交媒体、糖等丰裕疾病的成瘾性。
    Modern struggle is resisting addiction to social media, sugar, and other abundance diseases.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 专业化是昆虫的事；人类应该尝试生活中的一切。
    Specialization is for insects; humans should try everything in life.
  • 阅读应满足真正的好奇心，而非以读完书作为虚荣指标。
    Reading should satisfy genuine curiosity, not complete books as a vanity metric.
  • 幸福是一种选择；欲望是让自己不快乐直到得到想要的契约。
    Happiness is a choice; desire is a contract to be unhappy until you get what you want.
  • 要致富，必须拥有一部分企业；出租时间无法让你富有。
    To get rich, own a piece of a business; renting your time won't make you wealthy.
  • 现代斗争是抵制社交媒体、糖等富足疾病的成瘾。
    Modern struggle is resisting addiction to social media, sugar, and other abundance diseases.
  • 冥想是无为的艺术；平静来自内心，而非解决外部问题。
    Meditation is the art of doing nothing; peace comes from within, not solving external problems.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 全民基本收入是对非问题的非解决方案；自动化创造新就业。
    Universal Basic Income is a non-solution to a non-problem; automation creates new jobs.
  • AGI（通用人工智能）不会在我们有生之年到来；狭义AI是模式识别，而非通用智能。
    AGI is not coming in our lifetimes; narrow AI is pattern recognition, not general intelligence.
  • 社会主义只在小群体中有效；面对陌生人，资本主义能更好地协调激励。
    Socialism only works in small groups; with strangers, capitalism aligns incentives better.
  • 收入不平等源于不同选择，而非压迫；机会平等重要，而非结果平等。
    Income inequality is about different choices, not oppression; equal opportunity matters, not equal outcome.
  • 社交媒体平台在文化战争中选边站，正在缓慢自杀。
    Social media platforms are committing slow-motion suicide by picking sides in culture wars.
  • 生命的意义没有单一答案；你可以自己创造，这才是真正的自由。
    The meaning of life has no single answer; you get to make up your own, which is true freedom.
