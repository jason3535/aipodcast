本期速览 · Overview

布兰登·雅各比分享关于品味、AI 创造力以及 X 的决断文化的见解，他正启动自己的实践，帮助创始人超越“足够好”。

Brandon Jacoby shares insights on taste, creativity with AI, and the decisive culture at X, as he launches his own practice to help founders push beyond good enough.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 品味是知道何时打破规则、何时遵循模式。
    Taste is knowing when to break rules and when to follow patterns.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 品味是知道何时打破规则、何时遵循模式。
    Taste is knowing when to break rules and when to follow patterns.
  • AI工具使设计师能够构建自己的工具，释放创造力。
    AI tools empower designers to build their own tools, unlocking creativity.
  • 果断和判断力是核心设计技能，AI放大了它们。
    Decisiveness and judgment are core design skills, amplified by AI.
  • 优秀的设计平衡工艺与结果，既服务用户也服务业务。
    Great design balances craft and consequence, serving both users and business.
  • 早期设计依赖于信任、自信以及从不完美的工作开始。
    Early-stage design thrives on trust, confidence, and starting with imperfect work.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • AI并未消除对人类品味的需求，反而凸显了它。
    AI doesn't eliminate the need for human taste; it highlights it.
  • 设计师不应害怕AI，而应利用它构建定制工具。
    Designers should not fear AI; they should use it to build custom tools.
  • 最优秀的设计师往往是独立工作者，而非全职员工。
    The best designers are often independent, not full-time employees.
  • 对许多人来说，“足够好”可以接受，但卓越需要超越。
    Good enough is acceptable for many, but greatness requires pushing beyond.
  • 品味不仅是视觉的，还在于让高工艺为大众所及。
    Taste is not just visual; it's about making high craft accessible to many.
