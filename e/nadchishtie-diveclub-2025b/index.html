本期速览 · Overview

Lovable 设计主管分享招聘见解：寻找能端到端推进工作、用 AI 填补空白、在众多候选人中脱颖而出的通才设计师。

Lovable's Head of Design shares insights on hiring generalists who can run things end-to-end, use AI to fill gaps, and stand out in a sea of candidates.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 能端到端执行并用 AI 填补空白的通才最有价值。
    Generalists who own end-to-end execution and use AI to fill gaps are most valuable.
  • 作品集应优先展示工艺和意图，而非流程文档。
    Portfolios should prioritize craft and intentionality over process documentation.
  • 付费测试工作比任何面试环节都更能提供信号。
    Paid test work provides more signal than any other interview stage.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 能端到端执行并用AI填补空白的通才最有价值。
    Generalists who own end-to-end execution and use AI to fill gaps are most valuable.
  • 作品集应优先展示工艺和意图，而非过程文档。
    Portfolios should prioritize craft and intentionality over process documentation.
  • 招聘经理寻找高成长斜率、有内在驱动力和学习能力的人。
    Hiring managers look for high-slope individuals with intrinsic drive and learning aptitude.
  • 如果展示出用心和技能，副项目可以超越传统经验。
    Side projects can outweigh traditional experience if they demonstrate care and skill.
  • 带薪试工比其他任何面试环节都能提供更多信号。
    Paid test work provides more signal than any other interview stage.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 8-10人的跨职能小组并非最优；设计师与工程师的小团队更高效。
    Cross-functional squads with 8-10 people are suboptimal; small teams of designers and engineers work better.
  • 个人贡献者通道和专业化分类可能限制成长；通才常优于专才。
    IC tracks and specialist pigeonholing can limit growth; generalists often outperform specialists.
  • 作品集中过度解释过程可能暴露偏见或弱点，反而对候选人不利。
    Over-explaining process in portfolios can hurt candidates by revealing biases or weak areas.
  • 由于设定良好目标困难，指标和量化影响不被重点考量。
    Metrics and quantifiable impact are not heavily weighted due to difficulty in setting good goals.
  • 现在期望在核心工作流中使用AI；不采用就像早期忽视互联网。
    Using AI in core workflows is now expected; not adopting it is like ignoring the internet in the early days.
