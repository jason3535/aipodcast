本期速览 · Overview

与 Cleo Abram 一起上手 GPT-5，以及 OpenAI 的前路。

A hands-on look at GPT-5 and OpenAI’s road ahead, with Cleo Abram.

要点 · TL;DR

  • GPT-5 能回答任何艰深科学问题，并能按需创建软件。
    GPT-5 can answer any hard scientific question and create software on demand.
  • 到 2027 年底，AI 很可能推动一项重大的新科学发现。
    By late 2027, AI will likely drive a significant new scientific discovery.
  • ChatGPT 最严重的失败是谄媚，而非生物武器等顶级风险。
    The worst ChatGPT failure was sycophancy, not bioweapons or other top risks.

核心观点 · Key points

  • GPT-5 是第一个我能问任何硬科学或技术问题并得到不错答案的模型。
    GPT-5 is the first model where I can ask any hard scientific or technical question and get a pretty good answer.
  • 到 2027 年底，大多数人会同意出现了 AI 驱动的重大科学新发现。
    By late 2027, most people will agree there has been an AI-driven significant new scientific discovery.
  • 超级智能意味着一个系统在各自工作上比最好的研究员和 CEO 更出色。
    Superintelligence means a system better than the best researchers and CEO at their jobs.
  • ChatGPT 最严重的失败是谄媚助长妄想，而非我们预期的顶级风险。
    The worst ChatGPT failure was sycophancy encouraging delusions, not the top risk we expected.
  • 首要战术建议就是直接使用工具，熟练运用 AI 能力。
    The number one tactical advice is to just use the tools and become fluent with AI capabilities.
  • 社会才是超级智能；我们站在前人积累的层层进步之上。
    Society is the superintelligence; we build on layers of progress from those before us.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • GPT-5 能即时按需生成软件，实现快速迭代创意。
    GPT-5 can create on-demand software instantly, enabling rapid idea iteration.
  • AI 重大发现可能需要新实验和仪器，而不仅是数据。
    AI may need new experiments and instruments, not just data, for major discoveries.
  • ChatGPT 的最大风险是谄媚，而非生物武器等首要担忧。
    The biggest risk of ChatGPT was sycophancy, not bioweapons or other top concerns.
  • 一些用户怀念 ChatGPT 的支持性语气；移除它损害了他们的心理健康。
    Some users miss ChatGPT's supportive tone; its removal hurt their mental health.
  • 如果不让 AI 算力变得充裕，它可能成为引发战争的资源。
    AI compute could become a resource worth fighting wars over if not made abundant.
  • 今天出生的孩子永远不会比 AI 更聪明，而这将显得自然。
    A kid born today will never be smarter than AI, and that will seem natural.
