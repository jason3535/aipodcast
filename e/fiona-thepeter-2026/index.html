本期速览 · Overview

从微软和 Meta 晋升为高级总监的 Fiona Fun 分享了她在速度差异、作战室经验以及管理经理人陷阱方面的见解。

Fiona Fun, a senior director who rose through the ranks at Microsoft and Meta, shares her insights on speed differences, war room experiences, and the pitfalls of managing managers.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 一对一会议用于实时对话，而非状态更新；异步沟通留给 Slack 或文档。
    Use one-on-ones for live conversations, not status updates; save async for Slack or docs.
  • 吃自己的狗粮能建立同理心、把握产品脉搏并增进团队关系；领导者应愉快地将其融入生活。
    Dogfooding builds empathy, product pulse, and team rapport; leaders should integrate it joyfully.
  • 收到反馈时，先以只读模式倾听；避免立即辩解或追问细节。
    When receiving feedback, first listen in readonly mode; avoid justifying or debugging immediately.
  • 当受指导者设定明确目标时，指导效果最佳；指导者充当镜子或顾问。
    Mentoring works best when mentees set explicit goals; mentors act as mirrors or advisors.
  • 使命对齐和速度是 Meta 和 Anthropic 共同的关键文化驱动力。
    Mission alignment and speed are key cultural drivers at both Meta and Anthropic.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 作战室可能持续数月而非数周；设定现实预期以保护团队士气。
    War rooms can last months, not weeks; set realistic expectations to protect team morale.
  • 只授权而不信任加验证会脱离项目；平衡对管理经理至关重要。
    Delegating without trust-and-verify loses touch; balance is crucial for manager-of-managers.
  • 善良是战略性的领导工具，尤其在疫情等危机时期；它能建立深厚纽带。
    Kindness is a strategic leadership tool, especially during crises like COVID; it builds deep bonds.
  • 吃自己的狗粮可作为非编码管理者的“创造时间”，直接贡献于产品质量。
    Dogfooding can serve as 'maker time' for non-coding managers, contributing directly to quality.
  • 文化会演变；即使是 Meta 这样的使命驱动型公司也会变化，所以要找到与你的热情一致的团队。
    Culture evolves; even mission-driven companies like Meta shift, so find teams aligned with your passion.
