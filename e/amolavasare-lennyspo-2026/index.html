本期速览 · Overview

Anthropic 增长负责人 Amol Avasare 分享公司如何实现史上最快增长——14 个月内从 10 亿到 190 亿美元 ARR，以及他如何通过一封冷邮件获得这个职位。

Amol Avaisary, Head of Growth at Anthropic, shares how the company achieved the fastest growth in history, from $1B to $19B ARR in 14 months, and how he landed the role via a cold email to Mike Krieger.

要点 · TL;DR

  • Anthropic 在 14 个月内将年收入从 10 亿美元增至 190 亿美元，得益于模型的指数级改进。
    Anthropic grew from $1B to $19B ARR in 14 months via exponential model improvements.
  • 在用户引导中增加摩擦反而能提升转化率，因为帮助用户找到合适的产品。
    Adding friction in onboarding can boost conversion by helping users find the right product.
  • AI 可以自动化增长实验，但跨职能的利益相关者管理仍需人类。
    AI automates growth experiments but human stakeholder management remains essential.

核心观点 · Key points

  • Anthropic 的增长主要源于模型的指数级进步，而非单纯的增长团队优化。
    Anthropic's growth is driven by exponential model improvements, not just growth team optimizations.
  • 激活对 AI 产品至关重要；增加适当的摩擦有助于用户理解产品价值。
    Activation is critical for AI products; adding the right friction helps users understand product value.
  • 当产品价值呈指数增长时，增长团队应转向更大规模的投入。
    Growth teams should shift toward larger bets when product value grows exponentially.
  • AI 可以自动化增长实验，但跨职能利益相关者管理仍需人类。
    AI can automate growth experimentation, but cross-functional stakeholder management still needs humans.
  • 工程师从 Claude Code 等 AI 工具中获得最大杠杆，给 PM 和设计角色带来压力。
    Engineers gain the most leverage from AI tools like Claude Code, straining PM and design roles.
  • Anthropic 专注于编码和企业级市场，由领导层推动，是其成功的关键。
    Anthropic's focus on coding and B2B, driven by leadership, has been key to its success.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 在 onboarding 中增加摩擦通常能通过帮助用户找到合适产品来提高转化率。
    Adding friction in onboarding often improves conversion by helping users find the right product.
  • PM 应专注于培养工程师成为迷你 PM，而非亲自交付功能。
    PMs should focus on upskilling engineers as mini-PMs rather than shipping features themselves.
  • AI 可以识别团队间的错位，在问题出现前减少协调负担。
    AI can identify misalignment across teams, reducing coordination toil before it causes issues.
  • 增长团队应乐于放弃短期收益，优先考虑安全和品牌。
    Growth teams should be comfortable leaving money on the table to prioritize safety and brand.
  • Anthropic 对安全的关注是长期竞争优势，而非增长障碍。
    Anthropic's safety focus is a long-term competitive advantage, not a hindrance to growth.
  • 创伤性脑损伤通过强制约束和正念，可以提升产品管理技能。
    A traumatic brain injury can improve product management skills through enforced constraints and mindfulness.
