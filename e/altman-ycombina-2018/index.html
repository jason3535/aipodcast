减少认知负荷，选择重要之事

Minimize Cognitive Load and Choose What Matters

萨姆·奥尔特曼 Sam Altman · Y Combinator · 2018-11-08 · 约 36 分钟 · 原视频 ↗

本期速览 · Overview

Sam Altman 讨论了减少无关干扰带来的认知负荷的重要性，专注于真正重要的事情，以及通过年度反思确保自己在做正确的事情。

Sam Altman discusses the importance of minimizing cognitive load from unimportant distractions, focusing on what truly matters, and the annual ritual of reflection to ensure you're working on the right things.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 通过减少干扰、专注真正重要的事来降低认知负荷。
    Minimize cognitive load by cutting distractions and focusing on what truly matters.
  • 低成本探索，果断舍弃，避免延迟人生计划。
    Explore cheaply, cut brutally, and avoid the deferred life plan.
  • AGI将把价值从智力转向人际关系和人生意义。
    AGI will shift value from intelligence to relationships and purpose.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 最小化无关紧要之事的认知负荷；多数人在这方面做得很差。
    Minimize cognitive load from distracting things that don't matter; most people are bad at this.
  • 花时间探索，低成本尝试许多事，然后残酷地砍掉不奏效的。
    Take time to explore, try many things cheaply, then brutally cut what's not working.
  • 以合适的时间尺度聚焦重要问题；避免延迟人生计划。
    Focus on important problems with an appropriate time horizon; avoid the deferred life plan.
  • 与能让你更雄心勃勃、改变视角的人为伍。
    Surround yourself with people who make you more ambitious and shift your perspective.
  • 生产力系统应简单；专注和优先级排序是关键。
    Productivity systems should be simple; focus and prioritization are key.
  • AGI 将降低人类智能的溢价；在人际关系和为他人创造价值中寻找意义。
    AGI will reduce the premium on human intelligence; find purpose in relationships and creating value for others.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 多数人过于规避风险；真正的风险是在安全路径上浪费职业生涯。
    Most people are too risk-averse; the real risk is wasting your career on safe paths.
  • 工作间隙休一年假可能是最佳职业举措之一。
    Taking a year off between jobs can be one of the best career moves.
  • 延迟人生计划（先赚钱再追梦）几乎从不奏效。
    The deferred life plan (make money first, then pursue passion) almost never works.
  • 扑克和天使投资比大多数正式方法更能教会你商业。
    Poker and angel investing teach more about business than most formal methods.
  • 大重量训练比高强度有氧运动带来更快的身体进步。
    Heavy lifting provides faster physical progress than heavy cardio.
  • 人们能感知真实性和动机；你无法假装对长期目标的承诺。
    People can sense authenticity and motivation; you can't fake commitment to a long-term goal.
