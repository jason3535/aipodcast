本期速览 · Overview

Granola 联合创始人 Sam Stephenson 分享他们如何通过观察真实用户行为、避免自上而下的设计陷阱，找到了正确的问题来解决。

Sam Stephenson, co-founder of Granola, shares how they discovered the right problems to solve by observing real user behavior and avoiding top-down design traps.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 观察真实用户行为胜过询问他们认为自己做什么。
    Observing real user behavior beats asking what they think they do.
  • 从一个痛点开始，解决好，再扩展。
    Start with one pain point, solve it well, then expand.
  • 保护核心会议流程，保持简单无摩擦。
    Protect the core meeting flow; keep it simple and frictionless.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 观察用户真实行为比询问他们自认为的做法更能揭示问题。
    Observing real user behavior reveals more than asking what they think they do.
  • 从单一痛点入手，先解决好，再逐步扩展。
    Start with a single pain point, solve it well, then expand.
  • 转录是原始材料，而非最终产物；应聚焦于可操作的笔记。
    Transcripts are raw material, not the final artifact; focus on actionable notes.
  • 早期对用户进行手动引导能提供高质量的反馈，助力产品迭代。
    Hand-onboarding early users provides high-quality feedback for product iteration.
  • 坚决保护核心会议流程，保持简单无摩擦。
    Protect the core meeting flow ruthlessly; keep it simple and frictionless.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 实时AI笔记因需要信任检查反而会降低会议参与度。
    Real-time AI note-taking can reduce meeting presence due to trust checks.
  • 转录应故意设置访问障碍，以优先突出笔记。
    Transcripts should be intentionally hard to access to prioritize notes.
  • 用户常处于系统一的应激模式，而非理性的系统二思考。
    Users are often in system-one reactive mode, not rational system-two thinking.
  • 只添加功能而不删除会导致复杂化；减法才是关键。
    Adding features without removal leads to complexity; subtraction is key.
  • 将AI视为需要上下文的实习生，而非全知全能的笔记员。
    Treat AI as an intern needing context, not an omniscient note-taker.
  • UI中的空间导航激活更古老的大脑区域，比搜索更直观。
    Spatial navigation in UI activates older brain parts, making it more intuitive than search.
