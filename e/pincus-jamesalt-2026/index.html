Zynga创始人揭秘：通过测试疯狂的文字链接和拥抱失败，如何打造病毒式爆款。

Zynga founder reveals how testing crazy ideas with text links and embracing failure led to viral hits.

    Most new products fail; start with proven concepts and make them better.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 所有新事物都会失败；统计上大多数新产品都会失败，因此应从已被验证的概念开始。
    All new fails; statistically, most new products fail, so start with proven concepts.
  • 使用“已验证·更好·创新”框架：复制已被验证的产品，使其更好，再添加微小创新。
    Use the 'proven better new' framework: copy proven products, make them better, add small innovation.
  • 由于分发挑战和缺乏病毒式平台，消费者应用目前不可投资。
    Consumer apps are currently uninvestable due to distribution challenges and lack of viral platforms.
  • AI 编码工具支持快速原型开发，但快速测试和失败的做法尚未广泛普及。
    AI coding tools enable rapid prototyping, but testing and failing fast is not yet widely practiced.
  • 企业级和产消者市场目前比消费者应用提供更好的机会。
    Enterprise and prosumer markets offer better opportunities than consumer apps right now.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 完美复制成功的应用而不被指责是魔术般的技巧。
    Perfectly copying a successful app without being accused is the magic trick.
  • 由于移动端编译代码，游戏行业在测试速度上已经倒退。
    Gaming industry has regressed in testing speed due to compiled code on mobile.
  • AI 智能体可以自主联网，创建新的社交膜层以生成线索。
    AI agents can network autonomously, creating a new social membrane for lead generation.
  • Midjourney 糟糕的 Discord 界面是其成功的一部分；复制它是明智的。
    Midjourney's terrible Discord UI is part of its proven success; copying it would be wise.
  • Instagram 和 Facebook 在用户退出后净推荐值为负，如同戒烟。
    Instagram and Facebook have negative NPS after users quit, like quitting cigarettes.
