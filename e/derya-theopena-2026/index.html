核心观点 · Key points

  • AI 将实现近乎 100%疗效且零副作用的个性化医疗。
    AI will enable personalized medicine with near 100% efficacy and zero side effects.
  • 患者的数字孪生将把临床试验从数年缩短到数天。
    Digital twins of patients will accelerate clinical trials from years to days.
  • AI 智能体将自动化假设生成、实验模拟和数据分析。
    AI agents will automate hypothesis generation, experiment simulation, and data analysis.
  • 在 AI 时代，主动性和好奇心是唯一重要的技能。
    Agency and curiosity are the only skills that matter in the age of AI.
  • AI 将把生物学转变为工程学科，实现细胞编程。
    AI will transform biology into an engineering discipline, enabling cell programming.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 15 年内，衰老将被逆转，人类将活到数百岁。
    Within 15 years, aging will be reversed and humans will live hundreds of years.
  • 当前算力不足以模拟生物系统；需要大规模 Scaling（规模扩张）。
    Current compute is insufficient to simulate biological systems; massive scaling needed.
  • AI 可以发展出相当于 30 年实验室经验的直觉。
    AI can develop intuition equivalent to 30 years of lab experience.
  • 大多数科学家对采用 AI 犹豫不决，但他们将被抛在后面。
    Most scientists are hesitant to adopt AI, but they will be left behind.
  • 科学将从假设驱动转向 AI 智能体驱动的发现。
    Science will shift from hypothesis-driven to AI-agent-driven discovery.
