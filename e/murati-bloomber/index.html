本期速览 · Overview

米拉·穆拉蒂谈她的新实验室,以及构建可理解的 AI。

Mira Murati on her new lab and building understandable AI.

要点 · TL;DR

  • AI应实现实时、高带宽的协作，而非回合制交互。
    AI should enable real-time, high-bandwidth collaboration, not turn-based interaction.
  • 让人类参与循环是使AI与人类意图和价值观对齐的关键。
    Keeping humans in the loop is key to aligning AI with human intent and values.
  • 我们有能力引导AI发展走向协作，而非乌托邦或反乌托邦。
    We have agency to steer AI development toward collaboration, not dystopia or utopia.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 交互模型实现实时、高带宽的人机协作，而非回合制。
    Interaction models enable real-time, high-bandwidth human-AI collaboration, not turn-based.
  • 最先进的AI系统应成为思维工具，扩展我们思考的内容。
    The most advanced AI systems should be tools for thought, expanding what we think about.
  • 在AI进步过程中，让人类参与其中对于对齐意图和价值观至关重要。
    Keeping humans in the loop is crucial for aligning intent and values as AI advances.
  • 制度设计、透明度和制衡比个人品格更重要。
    Institutional design, transparency, and checks and balances matter more than individual character.
  • 未来并非注定；我们有能力引导AI发展走向协作。
    The future is not predestined; we have agency to steer AI development toward collaboration.
  • 前沿AI的快速进步带来波动，但长期可持续进展是关键。
    Compressing progress in frontier AI leads to volatility, but long-term durable progress is key.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 交互模型应基于时间而非轮次，以捕捉人类细微差别。
    Interaction models should be time-based, not turn-based, to capture human nuance.
  • ChatGPT最重要的影响是将AI带入公众意识，而不仅是技术。
    The most important impact of ChatGPT was bringing AI into public consciousness, not just technology.
  • 讨论过于关注个人品格，而对治理和透明度的关注不足。
    The conversation focuses too much on individual character, not enough on governance and transparency.
  • 预测乌托邦或反乌托邦过于简单化；我们在如何构建AI上有能动性。
    Predicting utopia or dystopia is oversimplified; we have agency in how we build AI.
  • 持久AI进步的道路是尽可能让人类参与循环，而非移除他们。
    The path to durable AI progress is keeping humans in the loop as long as possible, not removing them.
  • 将进展压缩到短时间内会导致好与坏的结果在几个月内出现。
    Compressing progress into short timeframes leads to both good and bad outcomes in months.
