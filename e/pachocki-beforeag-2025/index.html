本期速览 · Overview

OpenAI 首席科学家 Jakub Pachocki 和技术研究员 Shimon Cedor 分享了他们从波兰高中到塑造 AI 未来的旅程，探讨了前沿研究的挑战以及强大 AI 系统的深远影响。

Jakub Pachocki and Shimon Cedor, OpenAI's chief scientist and technical fellow, discuss their journey from high school in Poland to shaping AI's future, the challenges of pioneering research, and the profound implications of powerful AI systems.
