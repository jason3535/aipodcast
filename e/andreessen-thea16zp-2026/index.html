新媒体 vs 旧媒体：真实性与媒体培训的消亡

New Media vs Old Media: Authenticity and the Death of Media Training

本期速览 · Overview

探讨新媒体如何从受限渠道和虚假形象转向无限格式和真实个人品牌，并批评传统媒体培训。

A discussion on how new media has shifted from restricted channels and plastic personas to unlimited formats and authentic personal brands, with a critique of traditional media training.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 新媒体让品牌成为个人，真实性是新货币。
    New media makes the brand the person; authenticity is the new currency.
  • 旧媒体是防御性的，新媒体是进攻性的，需要直接讲故事。
    Old media is defensive; new media is offensive and requires direct storytelling.
  • 新媒体技能可习得，创始人必须亲自讲述自己的故事。
    New media skills are learnable; founders must tell their own story.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 新媒体拥有无限的格式和渠道；品牌现在就是个人。
    New media has unlimited formats and channels; the brand is now the person.
  • 真实性是关键：在公开场合说你会对朋友在午餐时说的话。
    Authenticity is key: say in public what you'd say to a friend over lunch.
  • 旧媒体是防御导向的；新媒体是进攻导向的。
    Old media is defense-oriented; new media is offense-oriented.
  • 创始人必须通过自己的渠道直接讲述自己的故事。
    Founders must tell their own story directly through their own channels.
  • 最有趣的故事涉及世界，而不仅仅是你的公司。
    The most interesting stories involve the world, not just your company.
  • 新媒体技能可以培养；这是一套可学习的技能。
    New media skills can be developed; it's a learnable skill set.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 旧媒体的规则是‘不要有趣’；这是你能做的最糟糕的事。
    Old media's rule is 'don't be interesting'; that's the worst thing you can do.
  • 你再也无法通过传统媒体获得一个好故事了。
    You cannot get a good story through traditional media anymore.
  • 传统媒体的‘向权力说真话’退化为‘权力决定真相’。
    Legacy media's 'speak truth to power' devolved into 'power to truth'.
  • 有人恨你是好事；这意味着你留下了印记。
    It's good to have people hate you; it means you made a mark.
  • 冲突有利于品牌建设；但要选对冲突。
    Fights are good for brand building; pick the right fights.
  • 企业品牌只在集中式媒体时代才有意义。
    Corporate brands only made sense in the centralized media era.
