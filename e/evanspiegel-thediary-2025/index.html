本期速览 · Overview

Snapchat 联合创始人埃文·斯皮格尔分享了他从童年组装电脑到拒绝扎克伯格 30 亿美元收购的历程，以及驱动他成功的核心原则。

Evan Spiegel, co-founder of Snapchat, shares his journey from building his own computer as a kid to turning down a $3 billion offer from Mark Zuckerberg, and the principles that drove his success.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 通过产生大量想法来最大化学习速度；最好的想法来自数量。
    Maximize learning rate by generating many ideas; the best idea emerges from quantity.
  • 友善培养创意文化，让人们无惧分享疯狂想法。
    Kindness fosters creative culture, enabling sharing of crazy ideas without fear.
  • 构建平台而非功能，使复制变得困难，维持竞争优势。
    Build a platform, not a feature, to make copying difficult and sustain competitive advantage.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 最大化学习速度；拥有好想法的最佳途径是拥有大量想法。
    Maximize your rate of learning; the best way to have a good idea is to have lots of ideas.
  • 善良是创意文化的关键要素，它让人们能够毫无顾虑地分享疯狂的想法。
    Kindness is essential for a creative culture; it enables people to share crazy ideas without fear.
  • 专注于构建平台或生态系统，而不仅仅是单一功能，以增加被抄袭的难度。
    Focus on building a platform or ecosystem, not just a feature, to make copying difficult.
  • 早期适当变现部分股份，让创始人能够无后顾之忧地全力冲刺。
    Take some money off the table early to allow founders to swing for the fences without personal risk.
  • 尽早将公司价值观融入招聘、晋升和绩效评估中，以维护企业文化。
    Embed company values into hiring, promotion, and performance evaluation early to maintain culture.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 拒绝 Facebook 30 亿美元的收购是正确的决定；他们热爱自己的产品并相信其未来。
    Turning down a $3 billion offer from Facebook was the right decision; they loved their product and believed in its future.
  • Snapchat 的设计团队只有 9 人且结构扁平，99% 的想法都不好，但剩下的 1% 推动了创新。
    Snapchat's design team of nine people is flat and generates 99% bad ideas, but that 1% drives innovation.
  • 内容审核并非审查制度；它是基于价值观的选择，旨在为用户创造舒适的环境。
    Content moderation is not censorship; it's a values-based choice that creates a comfortable environment for users.
  • Meta 抄袭 Snapchat 的功能令人沮丧，因为他们的产品让人不开心，而 Snapchat 则不然。
    Meta copying Snapchat's features is frustrating because their products make people unhappy, unlike Snapchat.
  • 最好的领导者是 T 型人才：在某一领域有深厚专长，同时对整个业务有广泛理解。
    The best leaders are T-shaped: deep expertise in one area and broad understanding of the whole business.
