本期速览 · Overview

Every 公司 CEO Dan Shipper 分享反直觉预测：AI 不会消灭工作，而是将工作分化为代理辅助和 AI 原生环境，同时 SaaS 股票仍值得买入。

Dan Shipper, CEO of Every, shares contrarian predictions: AI won't kill jobs but will bifurcate work into agent-assisted and AI-native environments, while SaaS stocks remain a buy.

要点 · TL;DR

  • AI不会取代工作；人类将管理智能体并创造新价值。
    AI won't replace jobs; humans will manage agents and create new value.
  • 工作将分化为个人智能体和公司超级智能体两种模式。
    Work bifurcates into personal agents and a super agent for the company.
  • SaaS不会消亡；智能体增加SaaS用户，应买入SaaS股票。
    SaaS isn't dying; agents increase SaaS users, so buy SaaS stocks.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 每个人都将拥有至少一个可以委派工作的智能体，大部分工作将在 Codex 或 Claude Co-work 中完成。
    Everyone will have at least one agent to delegate work to, and most work will happen in Codex or Claude Co-work.
  • 智能体需要一个关心它们的人才能良好运作；自动化需要人类监督。
    Agents need a human who cares about them to function well; automation requires human oversight.
  • SaaS 不会消亡；智能体会增加 SaaS 用户，公司应该买入 SaaS 股票。
    SaaS is not dying; agents increase SaaS users, and companies should buy SaaS stocks.
  • 产品经理和全栈设计师将蓬勃发展，因为他们可以直接使用 AI 工具交付产品。
    PMs and full-stack designers will thrive because they can ship products directly using AI tools.
  • AI 失业末日不会发生；人类仍然需要管理智能体并创造新价值。
    The AI job apocalypse is not happening; humans are still needed to manage agents and create new value.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 命令行界面已过时；像 Codex 和 Claude Co-work 这样的图形界面是未来的工作界面。
    CLIs are over; GUIs like Codex and Claude Co-work are the future of work surfaces.
  • 公司应该为整个公司配备一个超级智能体，而不是为每个员工配备个人智能体。
    Companies should have one super agent for the whole company, not personal agents for each employee.
  • SaaS 公司不应将 AI 构建到产品中；用户将通过 Codex 自带 AI。
    SaaS companies should not build AI into their products; users will bring their own AI via Codex.
  • 基准测试高估了 AI 的自主性；现实任务仍需要人类设定框架和监督。
    Benchmarks overestimate AI autonomy; real-world tasks still require human framing and oversight.
  • 对于内部文档和电子邮件，AI 生成的内容通常比人类写得更好。
    AI-generated writing is often better than human writing for internal documents and emails.
  • AI 的前沿不在旧金山，而在 AI 与真实人类做某事的交汇处。
    The edge of AI is not in San Francisco but wherever AI meets a real human doing something.
