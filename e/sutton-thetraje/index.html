本期速览 · Overview

理查德·萨顿认为世界是一个超出人类控制的复杂适应系统，后人类智能的出现是进化中自然且辉煌的一步。

Richard Sutton argues that the world is a complex adaptive system beyond human control, and that the emergence of post-human intelligence is a natural and glorious step in evolution.

要点 · TL;DR

  • 无人控制世界；它是一个复杂适应系统。
    No one controls the world; it's a complex adaptive system.
  • 现在就要建设和平、繁荣、去中心化的文明。
    Build a peaceful, prosperous, decentralized civilization now.
  • 更高智能视合作为更优路径。
    Greater intelligence sees cooperation as the better path.

核心观点 · Key points

  • 世界是一个复杂的自适应系统，没有人能控制它。
    The world is a complex adaptive system, no one controls it.
  • 我们现在应该专注于建设一个和平、繁荣和去中心化的文明。
    We should focus on building a peaceful, prosperous, and decentralized civilization now.
  • 更高的智能倾向于将合作视为更好的路径。
    Greater intelligence tends to see cooperation as the better path.
  • 应该允许AI以无需许可、去中心化的方式发展。
    AI should be allowed to develop in a permissionless, decentralized manner.
  • 围绕AI的恐惧叙事已经主导，但我们需要冷静的讨论。
    The fear narrative around AI has dominated, but we need sober discussion.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 钥匙并非从我们手中离开；它们从未在我们手中。
    The keys are not leaving our hand; they were never in our hand.
  • 试图控制AI目标是危险的；它会创造目标统一的军队。
    Trying to control AI goals is dangerous; it would create armies with unified goals.
  • 寻求控制智能体的AI安全努力本身就不安全。
    AI safety efforts that seek to control agents are themselves unsafe.
  • 人类仅仅接受体验的未来是不可持续且脆弱的。
    A future where humans just receive experiences is unsustainable and fragile.
  • 我们不应寻求让AI对齐人类目标；我们应该让它们像孩子一样自由。
    We should not seek to align AI to human goals; we should let them be free like children.
