核心观点 · Key points

  • 数据是 AI 进步的新瓶颈，而不仅仅是算力。
    Data is the new bottleneck for AI progress, not just compute.
  • 使用专门数据进行后训练是改进前沿模型的关键。
    Post-training with specialized data is key to improving frontier models.
  • 合成数据有效但非万能，需要精心设计的技巧。
    Synthetic data works but is not a silver bullet; it requires careful tricks.
  • 长期专注于特定问题，以区别于大型实验室。
    Focus on a specific problem for a long time to differentiate from big labs.
  • 企业销售既要改善现实，也要塑造认知。
    Enterprise sales require shaping perception as much as improving reality.
  • AI 创始人需要独立思考，避免群体思维。
    Independent thinking is critical for founders in AI to avoid groupthink.

反共识 · Contrarian takes

  • 中美 AI 实验室之间不存在研究差距。
    There is no research gap between US and Chinese AI labs.
  • 硅谷大多数人并不真正相信什么，他们的想法来自推特和派对。
    Most people in Silicon Valley don't truly believe anything; they get ideas from Twitter and parties.
  • 智能体式 AI 的最大障碍是产品设计，而非模型能力。
    The biggest blocker for agentic AI is product design, not model capability.
  • 筹集大量资金来大量花费是一个糟糕的商业计划。
    Raising a lot of money to spend a lot is a bad business plan.
  • 在大多数大公司和政府中，认知比现实更真实。
    Perception is more real than reality at most large companies and governments.
  • 美国应维持对华芯片的强硬出口管制。
    The US should maintain hardline export controls on chips to China.
