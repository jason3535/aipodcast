奥利维耶·戈德芒 Olivier Godement

OpenAI 平台(API)产品负责人 · Head of Product, Platform (API) at OpenAI

Olivier Godement 自 2023 年 5 月起担任 OpenAI 开发者平台(API)产品负责人,主导 Responses API、Agents SDK 等面向开发者的产品。他是法国籍产品负责人,加入 OpenAI 前曾在 Stripe 工作 8 年以上,毕业于巴黎政治学院与 ESSEC 商学院。

Olivier Godement is Head of Product for OpenAI's developer platform (API), which he has led since May 2023, overseeing products such as the Responses API and Agents SDK. A French product leader, he previously spent over eight years at Stripe and studied at Sciences Po and ESSEC.
