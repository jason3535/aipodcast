尤雷·莱斯科韦茨 Jure Leskovec

斯坦福大学计算机科学教授;Kumo.ai 联合创始人兼首席科学家 · Professor of Computer Science at Stanford University; Co-founder & Chief Scientist at Kumo.ai

尤雷·莱斯科韦茨是斯洛文尼亚裔美国计算机科学家、斯坦福大学计算机科学教授,同时是图机器学习创业公司 Kumo.ai 的联合创始人兼首席科学家。他是图神经网络与关系型深度学习的先驱,曾任 Pinterest 首席科学家,目前主导「AI 虚拟细胞」相关研究。

Jure Leskovec is a Slovenian-American computer scientist and professor of computer science at Stanford University, and co-founder and chief scientist of the graph machine-learning startup Kumo.ai. A pioneer of graph neural networks and relational deep learning, he previously served as chief scientist at Pinterest and leads work toward an AI Virtual Cell.
