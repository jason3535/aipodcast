丹·希珀 Dan Shipper

丹·希珀是 Every 的联合创始人兼 CEO,这是一家「应用 AI 实验室」兼媒体公司,既发布每日 AI 通讯,也开发多款 AI 软件产品。他撰写每周专栏 Chain of Thought,主持探讨人们如何使用 AI 的播客 AI & I;此前曾联合创办 Firefly,并于 2014 年将其出售给 Pegasystems。

Dan Shipper is the co-founder and CEO of Every, an applied AI lab and media company that publishes a daily AI newsletter and ships AI software products. He writes the weekly column Chain of Thought and hosts the AI & I podcast about how people use AI, and previously co-founded Firefly, which he sold to Pegasystems in 2014.
