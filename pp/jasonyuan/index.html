Jason Yuan is a designer known for AI-native interface design. He is the founder and CEO of Hivemind, a "social intelligence" company; previously he co-founded New Computer and led the design of Dot, a personal AI companion with long-term memory, and earlier designed intelligent interfaces on Apple's Human Interface team.
