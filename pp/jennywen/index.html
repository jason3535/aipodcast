温珍妮 Jenny Wen

Anthropic Claude 设计负责人;现任 Cursor 设计负责人 · Design Lead for Claude (Anthropic); now Head of Design at Cursor

Jenny Wen 曾在 Anthropic 领导 Claude 的设计工作,主导了 Claude.ai 改版与 Claude Cowork;此前她是 Figma 设计总监,带领 FigJam 与 Slides 团队,更早在 Dropbox、Square 和 Shopify 担任设计师。2026 年 7 月,她宣布离开 Anthropic,出任 Cursor 设计负责人。

Jenny Wen led design for Claude at Anthropic, where she drove the Claude.ai redesign and Claude Cowork; previously she was Director of Design at Figma leading the FigJam and Slides teams, and earlier a designer at Dropbox, Square, and Shopify. In July 2026 she announced she was leaving Anthropic to become Head of Design at Cursor.
