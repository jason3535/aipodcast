黛维·帕里克 Devi Parikh

Yutori 联合创始人兼联合 CEO · Co-founder & Co-CEO, Yutori

Devi Parikh 是 AI 网页智能体创业公司 Yutori 的联合创始人兼联合 CEO。她此前担任 Meta 生成式 AI 部门高级总监(至 2024 年 3 月),并曾任佐治亚理工学院副教授,以开创视觉问答(VQA)研究而知名。

Devi Parikh is co-founder and co-CEO of Yutori, a startup building AI web agents. She was previously a Senior Director in Generative AI at Meta (until March 2024) and an associate professor at Georgia Tech, and is known for pioneering Visual Question Answering (VQA).
