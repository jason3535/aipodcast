阿玛尔·雷希 Ammaar Reshi

Google AI Studio 产品与设计负责人 · Product & Design Lead, Google AI Studio

Ammaar Reshi 是一位设计负责人与 AI 创作者,目前在 Google DeepMind 领导 Google AI Studio(Gemini 的开发者平台)的产品与设计工作,此前曾任 AI 语音公司 ElevenLabs 的设计负责人。他以 AI 创意实验闻名,包括最早的 AI 生成童书之一《Alice and Sparkle》。

Ammaar Reshi is a design leader and AI creator who leads Product and Design for Google AI Studio (Gemini's developer platform) at Google DeepMind, having previously served as Head of Design at the AI voice company ElevenLabs. He is known for AI creative experiments, including "Alice and Sparkle," one of the first AI-generated children's books.
