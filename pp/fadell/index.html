托尼·法德尔是一位工程师、设计师与投资人,被称为「iPod 之父」,在苹果参与联合创造了 iPhone,并创立 Nest 且曾任其 CEO;2014 年 Nest 被谷歌以 32 亿美元收购。他曾是 General Magic 团队成员,如今是投资与顾问机构 Future Shape(Build Collective)的负责人,也是《纽约时报》畅销书《Build》的作者。

Tony Fadell is an engineer, designer, and investor known as the "father of the iPod" and co-creator of the iPhone at Apple, and the founder and former CEO of Nest, which Google acquired for $3.2 billion in 2014. He is a former member of the General Magic team, principal at his investment and advisory firm Future Shape (Build Collective), and author of the New York Times bestseller "Build".
