史蒂夫·鲁伊斯 Steve Ruiz

tldraw 创始人兼 CEO · Founder & CEO, tldraw

Steve Ruiz 是 tldraw 的创始人兼 CEO,这家伦敦创业公司打造了被广泛使用的 Web 无限画布 SDK,支撑着 Shopify、Google、Autodesk 及大量 AI 应用。他从美术出身转做设计工程,打造了将手绘草图变成可运行代码的爆款 demo「Make Real」。

Steve Ruiz is the founder and CEO of tldraw, the London-based startup behind a widely used infinite-canvas SDK for the web that powers products at Shopify, Google, Autodesk and many AI apps. A former fine artist turned design engineer, he created the viral "Make Real" demo that turns hand-drawn sketches into working code.
